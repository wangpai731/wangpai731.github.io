Bai Xiaochun was pale and trembling in the bottom of his heart. The sense of crisis that came from all over his body made him tremble constantly.

He had never been so afraid, whether he was lighting incense in the village, hearing thunder, being taken to the ten thousand snake Valley by Li Qinghou in the clan gate, or seeing all kinds of powerful creatures along the way.

In those days, although he was nervous, he knew he was It won’t die!

But now, he clearly knows that he Really will die!!

At the moment, he suddenly realized that there was no one around him. He was stunned for a moment and immediately turned around. He saw Du Lingfei and Hou Yunfei, who were surrounded by seven members of the Luochen family, hundreds of Zhang away.

We also saw the blood spurted by Du Lingfei. Anyone can see that Du Lingfei and Hou Yunfei can not hold on for long. They will be killed at any time.

Bai Xiaochun’s body suddenly stopped, standing there, looking at this scene, his body trembled more seriously, his eyes gradually bloodshot, he can strongly feel his body, his soul, are shouting to himself, let himself start to escape.

Even Bai Xiaochun has a certain degree of self-confidence. With his own speed, Du Lingfei and Hou Yunfei are blocking him. He has a great possibility Escape to heaven!

Once he escapes, he can live, he can continue to practice, he can pursue his ideal, and he will never die. Besides, the clan will not punish him, but will give him rewards.

But If he ran away like this, if he watched Du Lingfei and Hou Yunfei die, Bai Xiaochun would feel guilty all his life. He felt that his body was split into two consciousness at the moment. One was telling him to run away quickly, otherwise he would die. The other was to tell him that if he left the same door to escape, Bai Xiaochun would feel uneasy all his life!

Bai Xiaochun was short of breath and gave out meaningless growls. His forehead was full of blue veins. His body was shaking like a sieve. He didn’t think too much at the moment, and he couldn’t think about the consequences. He held his fist hard. He only knew a little

“Although I am afraid of death, I am You can’t just go away! " Bai Xiaochun suddenly hit his chest, his eyes were filled with blood. Yang Tian sent out a roar like thunder, and the whole person rushed out and ran straight Where Du Lingfei and Hou Yunfei are, go!

When he was too fast, there were gusts of wind, and the wind was blowing all over the place. Hou Yunfei’s body trembled. He saw Bai Xiaochun. He could not tell what color it was in his face. It seemed that he was surprised and relieved. He suddenly laughed. His eyes showed decisiveness. He was not protecting, but exerting all his strength to rush to the people who had landed the Chen family.

At the same time, Du Lingfei, who was almost penetrated by a sword and cut a bloody wound, also heard it. When she looked up, she saw Bai Xiaochun, whose eyes were red and frantic.

Du Lingfei’s whole person was like being bombarded by the thunder. She was stunned there, and her tears couldn’t be controlled. She didn’t know what emotion she was at the moment. She was moved, excited and shocked.

She knew that Bai Xiaochun was afraid of death, but now when she saw Bai Xiaochun come, she looked at the other party’s red eyes and trembling body. She could not imagine how much courage he needed to No more escape, choose to return.

“Let’s go, you idiot. Let’s go. Don’t come to death!” With tears in her eyes, Du Lingfei yelled to Bai Xiaochun. She didn’t notice a disciple of the Luochen family, who was on the sixth floor of condensing gas.

With a bang, Du Lingfei’s blood gushed out. Her eyes were blurred. Her body was thrown up. A flying sword was not far away. Now, under the control of another Luochen family member, Du Lingfei’s head roared.

When Bai Xiaochun saw this scene, he let out a stronger roar. His body speed broke out again in the original extreme. It seemed that his body had been pulled and directly cut through the sky. Unexpectedly, the whole person jumped away, driving his thin body with a huge impact force, forming a long rainbow. In the moment when the flying sword was about to approach Du Lingfei, he jumped away He appeared at Du Lingfei’s side, hugged it, turned a circle, clenched his fist with his right hand and blasted toward the flying sword.

With a bang, the flying sword trembled and was hit by Bai Xiaochun.

Du Lingfei, who is in Bai Xiaochun’s arms, is in a daze at the moment. Before she can react, Du Lingfei has been put aside by Bai Xiaochun. His body shakes and goes straight to the people who come to the Luochen family.

Bai Xiaochun’s eyes are as red as blood. He has long forgotten death and forgotten everything. There is only one idea in his mind: save Hou Yunfei, save Du Lingfei and kill all the people in front of him.

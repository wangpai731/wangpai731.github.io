Therefore, those who think they are strong will come.

Under the intense call of Bai Xiaochun, all the disciples here heard his voice one after another. Most of them listened to Bai Xiaochun’s advice and left with the apology and thanks given by Bai Xiaochun. “Something’s wrong. What happened to the barren mountains?” Shangguan Tianyou took a breath. He immediately thought of Bai Xiaochun’s oestrus pill, and his heart suddenly trembled.

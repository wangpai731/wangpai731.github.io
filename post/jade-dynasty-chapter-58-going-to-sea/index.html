Liubo mountain in the East China Sea, seven thousand miles into the sea, is the extreme east of the world, and farther away is the boundless sea.

It’s very remote here. It’s natural that there are no people here. Unexpectedly, just a few days after Zhang Xiaofan and others entered kongsang mountain, the demons suddenly emerged from all over the country. In the right way, all the major sects, led by Qingyun gate, Tianyin temple and burning incense Valley, discussed in a hurry.

At this time, the news suddenly came from the burning incense valley that a large number of people from the demon sect would gather in the remote place of Liubo mountain in the East China Sea. Why?

The so-called moral leadership is irreconcilable. People in the right way are filled with indignation. In a few days, they were mainly from the three sects. They sent their elite disciples to Liubo mountain in the East China Sea under the guidance of the elders with profound cultivation. Along the way, many decent people joined in, intending to clean up the demons and benefit the world.

Along the way, Zhang Xiaofan was interested in inquiring about how much he knew what had happened. His heart was filled with blood, and he was determined to go to the East China Sea.

But the way to the East is very far. The demon sect chose this place, but I was afraid that even though the central plains were rich, they were also deeply rooted in the three sects. It’s just that the righteous road is not prosperous now, and in the hearts of all righteous people, the words of righteousness are at the top of their heads, and those who are far away still rush to destroy it!

On the way, Zhang Xiaofan was in a hurry. Except for rest, he was flying in the sky with a fire stick. More than two days later, out of the sea, you can often see some small islands at the beginning. Another ten days later, when you fly far away, you can only see the blue sea and blue sky, the sky is high and the clouds are light.

It’s often flying day and night. The sea is clear and blue. If it’s not for the waves, it’s almost like a crystal clear and beautiful gem under his feet, but there’s no shadow of the island.

At this time, Zhang Xiaofan is in the middle of the air. The sea breeze came, cool on his face, but he was worried.

Today, he has been flying out of the East China Sea for ten days. This time, he did not find an island to rest for one day or two nights. Unexpectedly, I lost my way in the boundless sea.

But these days, he’s been sleeping and eating, and nothing else. He’s very good at flying in the sky. He’s not as frightened as before.

At the moment, he looked up at the sky, and looked down at the blue sea under his feet. He could not help but smile bitterly.

Zhang Xiaofan’s heart moved. At the beginning of the sea, he saw many of these seabirds, but when they flew far away, they could not reach them, and they could not see them any more. Unexpectedly, there are seabirds in the deep sea. It seems that there must be islands nearby.

Think about it. Zhang Xiaofan was excited and did not hesitate to fly towards the seagull. Boundless sea, boundless. In the distant horizon, the sky and the sea are the same, poetic and picturesque.

Overhead, the seagulls are singing and circling over the island, and the fresh sea breeze is blowing from the sea level, which is very cool. Zhang Xiaofan took a deep breath. In this desolate place, a sense of tiredness came up. He looked left and right. There was nothing strange. He found a clean place, lay down with his clothes, and soon fell asleep.

This sleep is quite fragrant. The island is quiet. Apart from the tide and sea breeze, there is no change. Naturally, no one will disturb it. Zhang Xiaofan sleeps until dusk, and then wakes up.

PS:

The words “Xi, Ma, Ni, Biao, Mi, Moo!” recited by Pu Zhi in the text, are known as the “Six-character Daming Mantra”, also known as the “Quan Yin inspiration mantra” in Buddhist classics. The Buddhist Sutra contains: This mantra is universal, the recitation can take off the dust, refresh the heart mirror, and reach the state of great joy and bliss. It is one of the most famous classic mantras among Buddhists.

The full text appendix is ​​as follows:

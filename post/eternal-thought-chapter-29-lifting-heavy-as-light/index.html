These two realms are the methods of enlightenment when practicing the skill of controlling tripod with Ziqi. If you can practice to the extreme, you can develop a kind of magic power called Ziqi Huading Ding.

In the second realm, it is even more rare to lift the tripod with light weight. As for the perfection of cultivation, there are even fewer miracles of Ziqi melting tripod.

In the war between Xu Baocai and him, he was mistakenly thought by the supervisors’ room and the huozao room that he had realized that he could lift a heavy weight lightly. In retrospect, Bai Xiaochun’s mind flashed away.

“The reason why I was thought to realize that lifting heavy weights like light is that I controlled the blade of the small wooden sword, but most of the reason was that the power of the small wooden sword was so powerful after it was refined.

In fact, I am not proficient in the manipulation of wooden swords, let alone lift weights lightly… " Bai Xiaochun frowned and sat cross legged in the courtyard. She looked down at the wooden sword. Her eyes were slowly numb, but blood gradually appeared.

A moment later, his right hand suddenly lifted up, and the small wooden sword flew out. He slashed hard in front of him. The wind howled, blowing up a lot of dust on the ground. Bai Xiaochun frowned more tightly. He pointed again with his right hand. This time, he didn’t mean the wooden sword, but the big stone just penetrated by the wooden sword outside the courtyard.

Under a finger, the big stone suddenly vibrates, slowly rises a foot, Bai Xiaochun’s aura is not stable, a bang, the big stone fell down again.

But Bai Xiaochun not only did not feel depressed, but his eyes became more and more bright. With persistence, he moved the aura in his body and pointed again.

Once, twice, three times

As time went by, half a month passed. In the past half a month, Bai Xiaochun tried to control the big stone almost every moment. The stone was more than three people high and weighed no less than seven or eight hundred jin. Even if Bai Xiaochun reached the fifth layer of condensate gas, it was not easy to control it thoroughly.

This is still because his internal cultivation is refined, so he can try it. If he changes the other five layers of condensate, it will only lift the big stone by several inches at most.

At present, after a month of unremitting efforts, Bai Xiaochun has long found that the biggest difficulty in controlling the stone is not the lack of aura, but the instability in the control. He often has aura in his body, but he interrupts himself.

“The key to controlling things is to keep the silk thread flowing at a steady speed.” White small pure eyes in a flash of light, eyes bloodshot diffuse, hoarse murmur.

This is just like the noodles made by ordinary people. If the speed of pulling is too fast, the noodles will be broken. If it is too slow, it can not be pulled out too long. Only by grasping an inch of strength can we be able to do what we want.

If a monk wants to control things to an extraordinary level, he needs to keep it steady. In this way, the difficulty will naturally increase.

“I’ve learned that lifting heavy things like light doesn’t mean literally. Controlling heavy things has a light and spiritual feeling. It’s just a representation. The real meaning is not the manipulation of things, but the manipulation of aura.”

“The total amount of Reiki in the five layers of condensate gas is the word” heavy “, and turning it into a thin but continuous silk thread is the word” light “. If it can be done, it is like lifting heavy as light, and the external performance is the speed after being able to handle it well!” Bai Xiaochun looked excited. After he figured out the problem, he raised his right hand, and immediately the big stone in the distance vibrated and was lifted suddenly.

It was as if an invisible hand grasped the big stone. With a cry after it was lifted off, he went straight to Bai Xiaochun. But in the middle of the air, he stopped and hit the yard, raising dust.

Bai Xiaochun did not feel discouraged, and continued to try, until half a month later, he found that in any case, he could not seem to be able to make his aura always keep a steady silk thread.

Sometimes, even if it is really done, but because the stone is too heavy, the aura silk thread is still unstable and disconnected in the flow.

However, if you want to control a small wooden sword, there is no such problem. Because its weight is not as big as that of big stone, Bai Xiaochun obviously feels much more relaxed in the control, and with a month’s practice, the speed is a little faster.

As a matter of fact, Bai Xiaochun was not satisfied with the fact that he was lifting heavy weights lightly. His eyes turned red and he bit his teeth fiercely. That kind of ferocity came into being.

“I don’t believe it!” White small pure right hand pinches a Jue, this big stone unexpectedly flew to his head.

Bai Xiaochun’s forehead exudes cold sweat. He looks at the big stone on his head in fear, and tries his best to maintain the invisible spiritual silk thread. If it is broken, after the big stone falls down, although it can’t kill him, it will also cause severe pain.

This time, there was a lot more time to insist. But half an hour later, with a bang, Bai Xiaochun screamed. The big stone fell down. For a long time, the rock shook and was pushed away. Bai Xiaochun climbed out of it.

He has skin protection, no injury, but the pain makes him grinning.

But his hard work is stronger. After a month’s continuous efforts, the big stone has gradually changed from falling many times a day to once a day. At the end of the day, even Bai Xiaochun can do it once a day without failure.

And the big stone was slowly raised by him. At most, it reached a height of more than ten Zhang. If it was smashed, even Bai Xiaochun would be pale.

But only in this way can his mind be highly concentrated.

Gradually, he was able to keep the aura steady from the beginning to the end without interruption. However, he was not satisfied, so he no longer sat on his knees, but moved and maintained the aura as he moved.

As the difficulty increased, the roar in the yard rang again and again.

As time went by, when there were only three days left before Li Qinghou’s Xiangyun mountain Xiaobi, Bai Xiaochun’s yard was filled with a stone of seven or eight hundred jin in mid air. His figure moved rapidly in the courtyard, and he really achieved the degree that the stone remained motionless when he kept moving.

After moving for a long time, Bai Xiaochun stood in front of the wooden house and laughed. With a wave of his right hand, the stone in the air flew out of the yard and landed in the same place.

Bai Xiaochun pinches the Jue, and the small wooden sword flies out. It is still a simple cut forward, but the speed is so fast that it is already blurred. It is more than several times stronger than before.

Bai Xiaochun’s face was full of joy, and he made a quick trick.

The extreme speed, combined with the power of the wooden sword, immediately reached an amazing degree. For a moment, countless small wooden swords appeared in the yard, and the sword spirit was flying.

Finally, with his small sleeve swinging, all the imaginary shadows of wooden swords disappeared. Only one existed. He went straight to Bai Xiaochun and disappeared in his storage bag.

“This time, we should be able to enter the top five.” Bai Xiaochun took a deep breath. Although he still didn’t feel sure, the time was approaching. He could only sit on his knees and keep himself in the best condition.

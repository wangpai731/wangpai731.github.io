Qingyun Mountain, xiaozhufeng.

The dark clouds, like dark clouds, can only be seen from the sky. Far away from the horizon, faintly came the rumbling thunder. I don’t know whether there will be more violent wind and rain coming.

Lu Xueqi, one of the disciples of Qingyun gate who went to southern Xinjiang, has been back for several days. Lu Xueqi, one of the disciples, went back to xiaozhufeng after meeting the elders of the school. She never appeared again. Even the undercurrent caused by the mysterious disappearance of daoxuan immortal and Tian in Qingyun gate seems to have never left her mind.

The precipitous and beautiful xiaozhufeng is still as calm as it has been for thousands of years. In this stormy night, all over the mountains and fields are still singing the sound of sand and bamboo waves and gazing at the people at the top of the mountain.

The cottage is full of green lights and candles.

The door closed gently, but the window was still half open. The wind and rain came quietly in the mountain. The rain powder flew into the room from time to time, wetting the window sill made of bamboo, and slowly condensed into water drops, slipping down quietly, leaving a trail of water marks. The wind from afar gently shakes the window and makes a soft “creak” sound in this silent rainy night.

The candlelight on the table in the room flickered and disappeared. It seemed that it would be blown out several times. However, it was always struggling until the mountain breeze weakened, and slowly recovered to light again.

There was no other light in the night, and a shadow shrouded a little farther away from the candle.

Lu Xueqi sat under the lamp and watched the candle in silence.

Qingdeng, Hongyan, in such a night, as if the condensation of melancholy, silently engraved in the time, but do not know, and how much time, can be retained?

Outside the door, a light step rang from afar, Lu Xueqi’s head moved slightly. A mountain breeze came from the window. The candle on the table swayed and waned, and her hair on her temples fluttered gently with the wind.

The door, let out a low sound. It was pushed away. The wind and rain outside, suddenly loud, as if the wind suddenly increased, will rush into the house, fortunately. After that moment, the visitor had entered the room, turned back and closed the door, which also cut off the wind and rain outside the house, and restored the peace in the house.

Lu Xueqi stood up, slightly lowered her head and said, “elder martial sister, why are you here?”

Lu Xueqi looks up to Wenmin. She has a smile on her mouth. Her eyes are soft and full of care.

Lu Xueqi shook her head and said, “elder martial sister, what do you say? I dare not blame the master in the slightest. I dare not go to see the master. I just know that I am not good, and I am afraid that I will only make master angry and upset.”

Wen Min is stunned for a moment and looks at Lu Xueqi. After a long time, her face is complicated. She just sighs and stands up.

At the moment, in the distance of the sky, suddenly a flash of lightning flashed, followed by a thunderbolt. The sound was like a split silk, but it seemed to reverberate on the top of the head, and the echo was curling, which did not disperse for a long time.

The wind outside seems to be tightening up a bit.

Wen Min frowned, went to the window, took a look outside and said, “look at the sky, it’s like the rain is going to rain again.”

Lu Xueqi stood up and slowly walked to the window. Standing beside Wen Min, she looked out. In the night, two slim figures stood side by side, gazing at the dark night and endless wind and rain.

In the distance, the sound of sand and bamboo waves and rain beating bamboo leaves is coming. For a while, I don’t know whether it is quiet in this quiet, both of them are speechless.

After a long time, Wen Min inhaled deeply and said with a smile, “well, we haven’t seen the rain like this for a long time, have we?”

Lu Xueqi said with a smile: “yes, in fact, I also remember that when I was a child, I went up the mountain. At the beginning, I was taken care of by elder martial sister. At that time, I was not sensible. Whenever there was a rainy night and thunder roared, I was particularly afraid.

"

She turned her head slowly. Her eyes were soft and said in a low voice: “every time, it’s the elder martial sister. You take me with you and sit by the window to watch the rain. Tell me not to be afraid.”

Wen Min shakes her head and laughs. She reaches out and touches Lu Xueqi’s long, soft hair on her shoulder. Suddenly, she says, “in a flash, you’ve grown up.”

Lu Xueqi felt Wen Min’s palm and gently patted it on her shoulder, as if from there, there was a little warmth.

After a moment’s silence, Lu Xueqi looked at the elder martial sister and said, “elder martial sister, if you have anything to say, just tell me!”

Wen Min was stunned for a moment. At last, he gave a wry smile and said, “I know you’ve never been smart enough to hide anything from you…”

She paused for a moment and said, “younger martial sister, in fact, your intelligence is far better than that of my elder sister. But how can you not see through it and understand it clearly, and you will suffer in vain?”

Lu Xueqi’s smile at the corner of her mouth slowly disappeared. Instead, she had a familiar and indifferent look. However, in the face of Wen Min, she no longer had that cold feeling.

“I’m not bitter!” Lu Xueqi looks out the window at the night, so quietly.

Wen Min looks at her in astonishment. Lu Xueqi’s eyes are far away. She doesn’t know where to look in the deep night. But her tone is no longer clear: “I’ve never suffered, elder martial sister. Just now… " Lu Xueqi, smiling in a low voice, seemed to be deep in her heart and said, “if I want my whole life to be merciless and loveless, and if I want my heart to be immortal, then I will live forever and be immortal.

Lu Xueqi was stunned for a moment, then turned around and said, “although I don’t want to see the master, if I go, it will probably make her angry.”

Wen Min shook his head and said, “it’s Shifu who asked me to call you in private today. What I’ve done is business. Don’t worry.”

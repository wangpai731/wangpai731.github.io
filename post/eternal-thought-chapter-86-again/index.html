Bai Xiaochun was frightened all the way. He ran down the mountain or even out of the zongmen. He wandered around for a long time in the city outside the Zongshan gate of Lingxi.

“What to do.” Bai Xiaochun has a sad face. He is deeply aggrieved. When he thinks of it, it is clear that the bird came by himself and took the pill. But now he can’t explain it clearly, and elder Zhou is unreasonable

“But I seem to be First? " When Bai Xiaochun was worried, he suddenly thought of something and was stunned for a moment.

“Well, as my younger brother, I didn’t want to compete with those younger generation.” Bai Xiaochun coughed and felt a little complacent at the bottom of his heart. However, he thought that the first word was that he needed to fight against Tianjiao on the north bank, so he was depressed again.

After wandering around the city for a long time, until late at night, Bai Xiaochun pondered that she could not but go back, so she took out the jade slips and sent a letter to Xu Baocai.

Let Xu Baocai go to his yard to see if elder Zhou is blocked there.

After a long time, when he saw the sound of the jade slips sent back by Xu Baocai, he felt a little relieved. He went back to the Mountain Gate carefully and quickly went up Xiangyun mountain. From a distance, he saw only Xu Baocai outside the yard. After careful observation for a long time, he quickly approached.

As soon as Xu Baocai saw Bai Xiaochun, he immediately showed excitement in his eyes. He was about to speak and was pulled into the yard by Bai Xiaochun.

“Uncle Bai, don’t worry. Elder Zhou was called away by the headmaster at that time…” Xu Baocai opens his mouth in a hurry. He looks at Bai Xiaochun and is crazy in his eyes.

“When he was in the fire kitchen, he kept a secret and even came up with the idea of selling the number of disciples of the outer gate. Behind the inner door, he was even smaller than the first, causing a small stir. The most amazing thing was that he came back alive, glorifying the identity of the disciples and the dignity of the headmaster and younger martial brother. Now he is the first one in the qualification war He must be a man in the future

Xu Baocai saw with his own eyes the whole process of Bai Xiaochun’s surpassing everyone and becoming the number one, which has shocked most of the day. In his heart, he has regarded Bai Xiaochun as an amazing person, especially recalling the scenes of past events. He has more and more respect for Bai Xiaochun, and immediately clasped his fist and deeply worshipped him.

“Congratulations to Uncle Bai, who became the first person in the qualification war on the South Bank of China!”

Bai Xiaochun looked around in the yard. She felt relieved. She looked up at the direction of the old cave around the top of the mountain. She swore to herself that when she got to the foundation, she would stew and eat the bird that had framed her!

At the moment, hearing Xu Baocai’s words, Bai Xiaochun turns her head and looks at Xu Baocai and gives a dry cough.

“This seat is a person who is indifferent to fame and wealth, like white clouds in the sky. I didn’t want to compete with those younger generation for this false name. However, it was not the right time for Zhou Zhanglao to appear here.” Bai Xiaochun light mouth, put out a pair of senior master’s appearance, seems to be feeling.

If it’s the usual, Bai Xiaochun says so. Although Xu Baocai doesn’t dare to show any expression on the surface, he must despise him at the bottom of his heart. But now, Bai Xiaochun is already the Tianjiao in his eyes, so he speaks quickly.

“Uncle Bai has always kept a low profile, but even if you are so arrogant, even if you are so low-key, you will be able to wave your hand inadvertently, and you will shine with attention!”

Bai Xiaochun’s heart was full of enthusiasm, but he was still reserved on the surface. However, he was reluctant to let Baocai say more words in his heart. So when he looked at Xu Baocai, he looked appreciative and encouraged.

Xu Baocai felt Bai Xiaochun’s encouragement and spoke more excitedly.

“This time, uncle Bai, on behalf of our south bank and the north bank, will surely sweep away the weakness of our south bank for many years, and will certainly shock the north bank.”

“The five Tianjiao on the north bank are said to be the strongest under the foundation. They are ferocious and arrogant. This time let them understand that the real Tianjiao is on the South Bank of our country, in Xiangyun mountain!” Xu Baocai was excited.

“Five Tianjiao?” Bai Xiaochun looks as usual, but his heart jumps.

It is said that he can control five fierce beasts at the same time. There was a Ning Qi San Xiu that provoked him. He chased him for three months and finally controlled the five Beast, devour each other alive for three days, and only bones are left in the end

“Only bones left?” Bai Xiaochun was shocked.

It is said that his body is full of insect eggs, which is extremely strange. Those who fight against him often end up crawling all over the body, burrowing into the body and swallowing the flesh and blood. Some people say that he is the first day proud on the north bank… " Xu Baocai told Bai Xiaochun what he knew. Bai Xiaochun was shocked by the news.

He found that Tianjiao on the north bank seems to be much more vicious than the south bank. Although the north bank mainly controls animals and inevitably contacts with blood, the gap is too large.

This is a disgrace to us…” Xu Baocai was talking excitedly. He suddenly found that Bai Xiaochun’s face was not right. He was stunned for a moment and looked at Bai Xiaochun.

“Uncle Bai, what’s the matter with you?”

Bai Xiaochun quickly raised her chin and spoke solemnly.

“As a member of the south bank, I was furious to hear that I was so on the south bank!”

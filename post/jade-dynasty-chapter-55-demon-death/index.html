In the fiery underground caves, the heat waves rolled, and the red magma under the platform kept tumbling and bursting from time to time.

Zhang Xiaofan only felt that breathing seemed to be gradually becoming more difficult, and it seemed that the air sucked into his lungs was also hot. In this sense, wherever you take a step, you will step out of Mars. The white six-tailed fox in front is still lying there quietly. It seems to be very enjoyable to see.

He stood on the spot, looking at the front, hesitated for a moment, and finally walked forward, at the same time, he subconsciously clenched the burning stick in his hand.

The platform is long and narrow, extending all the way to the depths of the magma lake. As Zhang Xiaofan got closer and closer, the surrounding temperature became more and more hot, almost to the point of being unbearable.

I don’t know if it’s this scorching temperature or some other reason. Zhang Xiaofan’s throat is very dry, but he doesn’t dare to be distracted at all. He fixes his eyes on the fox.

He walked three steps away from it and stopped.

The distance was closer, and the sight was more real. He was suddenly shocked in his heart. This is indeed a beautiful fox, with pure white fur from top to bottom, especially in this place like flame hell, it is also like snow. In general, don't say that there is a stray hair, even if there is no trace of scorching.

However, its eyes are closed, and there is a slight wrinkle between the two eyes, as if there is a trace of pain, hanging between its eyebrows.

Zhang Xiaofan looked at it, but his mind flashed countless thoughts. From Xiaochi Town to the present, all he heard was that there was a "three-tailed demon fox" entrenched in harm. But looking at the fox in front of him, it was obviously different from the three-tailed demon fox just fighting.

One person and one fox, just confront each other. There was no sound around, only some sounded as if the magma lake had existed for thousands of years. It seems so far away.

In a farther place, the six-tailed white fox also frowned slightly, and looked at the Yongdao.

“Purple gas into tripod!” Hou Yunfei and Du Lingfei, at the same time, exclaimed. Both of them showed a stronger shock.

Du Lingfei, in particular, has already mastered the difficulty of lifting a heavy vessel like a light, and clearly knows the difficulty of turning the purple gas into a tripod, which is a magic power that few people can master on the south bank, even in the purple tripod mountain.

In the roar, the huge tripod was directly hit by the fierce ghost, and the ground was shaken for a moment. The ghost’s body broke down in an instant and turned into countless black gas, spreading to all directions, revealing the dying Chen Yue in it.

Chen Yue’s blood spurted out and his body fell to the ground. He looked at the dissipated tripod bitterly and murmured.

“Ziqi Huading… " After that, he struggled to look at Bai Xiaochun. His body did not move, and he died. He used to use the secret method. He had already suffered five pains and seven injuries. At the moment, he was broken by the purple Qi tripod, and even the fierce ghosts were broken. How could he continue to live.

Until he died, he opened his eyes and looked at Bai Xiaochun.

Seeing Chen Yue’s death, Bai Xiaochun’s body relaxed at once. He consumed too much aura in his body, so that his head was dizzy. He stood there shivering and his body was shaking. His face was pale. Some people couldn’t believe that he had killed everyone.

Looking back on the scene just now, Bai Xiaochun only felt a sweet taste in his mouth and the blood overflowed again.

“I’m bleeding I I was almost killed Bai Xiaochun felt pain all over her body, especially when her shoulder was lifted up. Her skin was scorched in many places. The pain made her think of the previous battle and shivered.

“I Why did I come back Just a moment ago, I was not careful, maybe I lost my life Bai Xiaochun has been safe for a small part of my life. How could I be impulsive this time… " When Bai Xiaochun was afraid of some regrets, he suddenly came across a delicate body with amazing elasticity, concave and convex, even with the delicate fragrance of a virgin, and rushed directly to his arms. Bai Xiaochun coughed, and his heart was quite memorable. He said that Du Lingfei was indeed one of the five beauties on the south bank. His figure alone was enough to stand out in the world.

At the moment, Hou Yunfei looks strange. He coughs and looks at Bai Xiaochun with a smile.

“Younger martial brother Bai, there will be plenty of time to ponder. The next group of pursuers will be sent out by the falling dust family. This time, it is estimated that they will be the strongest except the founder of Zhuji. We should seize the time to escape.”

When Bai Xiaochun heard this, his heart trembled. Just now these people, he fought everything to win. At the thought that the other party would send out more strong men like Chen Yue, Bai Xiaochun shivered and looked pale. After looking around, she shrunk his neck and nodded quickly.

However, Du Lingfei did not feel disgusted, but felt lovely. So he also followed the past. Bai Xiaochun didn’t look at it carefully and threw it into his arms. At the moment, he couldn’t help shivering. The only thought in his mind was to run for his life.

All the members of the Luochen family were excited when they saw the young man one by one. They all bowed their heads to see him.

“Heng’er, as the young master of my Luochen family, you are the strongest one in the family except for my husband.

The plain courtyard is just like the one outside. It is simply a room against the mountain wall. The middle path is paved with green bricks and leads to the door. There are grass on both sides. It seems that no one has taken care of it seriously. Weeds have grown in many places.

Different from the outside, there is a heavy black curtain hanging on the door of this room. Besides this door, there seems to be no other windows or other exits.

Ghost Li looked at this ordinary and ordinary hut, a burst of thirst in his throat, but his hands were involuntarily clenched. He looked at the master Puhong, but he could see that there was a very complicated look on his face, which was like regret, pain, and indescribable. He was also looking at the small door, in a daze.

For a time, there was no one to speak. In the silence, there was only a low chirp of insects in the weeds beside me. I didn’t know what was calling.

For a long time, master Pu Hong sighed softly and said, “let’s go in!”

The muscles on his face twitched and whispered, “OK.”

Master Pu Hong slowly stepped forward, stretched out his hand, opened the curtain, and pushed the door open with a squeak.

The faint sound came from the rotor on the door. I don’t know how many days no one opened the door. It was heavy and desolate.

A cold air suddenly rushed out of the house. Although the ghost was still standing outside the door, he was shocked by the cold air and shivered with his practice. In this small room, it seems to be the coldest place in the world.

GUI Li frowned and hesitated. At this moment, the voice of master Pu Hong came from behind the curtain and said, “come in, little benefactor.”

He took a deep breath, shook his head, stretched out his hand to open the curtain and stepped in.

The curtain fell slowly, and the door creaked again. Close it gently.

In the small courtyard, once again calm, the figure of FA Xiang came slowly from the front and looked at the flat and unadorned cottage. The Buddha was gently chanted in his mouth, but he bowed down and worshipped. His face was solemn and solemn.

The curtain was down and the wooden door closed because there were no windows. The room was in darkness.

The chilling chill came from all directions in an instant, as if countless cold steel needles were going to pierce into the skin.

At the beginning of his serious illness, he had a few cold wars. However, he was not a mortal after all. He gradually got used to it after all. Although the cold can not enter the body, but that piercing cold, still very uncomfortable.

This hut on Xumi mountain. It seems to be colder than that of the Arctic ice field.

GUI Li was stunned. When he was in doubt, he only heard a low sigh from the crowd in front of him and said, “younger martial brother, we’ve come to see you. This man, you’ve wanted to see for a long time!”

His voice was deep and strange, and the chill in the room was suddenly a little bit colder, almost freezing human blood into ice.

Then, a glimmer of light. White with a slight silver light, slowly from the front of the people and ghosts, the end of the hut, lit up.

The light was as light as snow. First, a wisp of bloom, and then at the edge of the light slowly lit up another silver shimmer, but also close to it, integrated, and then one by one glimmer of light successively lit up, gradually see. It’s a disc shape about one foot square.

The light was soft, pure white as snow. The light could not rise more than a foot high. At the end, it seemed to turn into snowflakes and white fireflies. It danced gently and fell slowly, like a dream.

Then, the continuous light, slowly merging, gradually bright, ghost and Pu Hong only heard a light roar rising in the room, the clear voice was pleasant, the white light was great, and the instant light was shining, illuminating the whole room.

It seemed that the heart of the Buddha stopped for a moment, even the heart of the Buddha stopped.

He just stood there like a stiff icicle, staring into the deep light. There was no other thought in his mind, only two words echoed

Puzhi!

The light is like snow, brilliant flow, from a disc of pure white as jade, and at the same time, it is cold. On that square disc, there was a man sitting on it. It was the man who changed Zhang Xiaofan’s life and made today’s ghostly and unforgettable person Puzhi.

From a distance, Puzhi’s face is lifelike. Although the skin looks pale and has no trace of vitality, it does not show any signs of dryness after careful observation.

Even, he was still the compassionate and peaceful old monk in Zhang Xiaofan’s memory. There was no change at all, but there was a faint pain between his looks.

Except for the body.

Puzhi’s body, somehow, was twice as small as the original. It was because of this that he sat on the pure white jade plate. He wanted to come to the room with cold air, but he didn’t see any ice piled up. Most of the reason lies in this exotic treasure. Of course, Puzhi’s remains can be kept for such a long time, mostly because of this exotic treasure.

However, he could not think of so many more in his mind. The monk sitting on the jade plate was clearly engraved in the bottom of his heart. For more than ten years, he did not forget.

Hate, right?

Is that right?

Sometimes his mind was empty, sometimes like a storm, thunder and lightning roaring, all kinds of pain, all kinds of gratitude and resentment, but for a moment they all spread to his heart!

That kind monk saved his life and taught him how to treat him like a son. But it was this seemingly merciful monk who ruined his life and made him suffer day and night, like falling into the abyss of hell

Gratitude and resentment, originally thought only in the heart, but now today, actually saw his face.

Some of them couldn’t stand, dizzy, and fell to the side. At this time, a gentle and warm hand reached out from the side and held him. At the same time, a familiar breath was the Buddhist Dharma mahabhabhabhabhabhabhabhabhabhabhabhabhadra, which came from the palm of that hand. “Amitabha, little benefactor, don’t be too excited. Take care of yourself.” The peaceful voice of master Pu Hong. It’s coming from the side.

GUI Li such as awakened from the dream, a bite teeth, deep breath, let go of Pu Hong’s hand. He stood up straight again. However, his eyes never left Puzhi’s face. In the dim light, Pu Zhixiang and his face. The look of pain seemed more profound.

He sighed softly.

His eyes were heavy, he turned his head and looked forward to Puzhi. May you lay down your sin and be reborn as soon as possible. Amitabha! "

He closed his arms, saluted Puzhi’s body, and then went straight out. At the moment when he was about to go out, he said, “little benefactor, I think you want to stay alone with younger martial brother Puzhi! I’m in the meditation room in front of me. If you need anything, come and see me. "

GUI Li didn’t speak and seemed to be deaf. At the moment, only the Puzhi monk in the dim light was in his eyes.

Ghouli slowly, slowly moving steps, bit by bit to the wisdom of the past.

GUI Li slowly walked to him, staring at Puzhi, his hands slowly clenched, and his nails were deeply embedded in his flesh, but finally he was released.

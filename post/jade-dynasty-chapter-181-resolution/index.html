The morning bell is long and the evening drum is heavy. The Xumi mountain is bathed in the misty clouds. From the rising sun to the dusk, the sky changes and the white clouds and dogs roll by. Time never stops for anyone.

Tianyin temple is majestic and majestic. It stands on Xumi mountain like a compassionate giant looking at the world. The setting sun is like blood, reflecting the sunset glow in the western sky. Looking from afar, there seems to be a thin layer of golden light on the edge of the cloud, which is very beautiful.

Zhang Xiaofan came up to the mountain and came to the familiar bamboo forest, but he saw the mountains full of verdant mountains, cascading layers, mountain breezes passing by, the bamboo sea undulating, like the waves of the sea, extremely magnificent, and his heart suddenly widened.

He took a deep breath of fresh air in the mountains, moved around, and walked into the bamboo forest with a hatchet. The place he went to at this time was different from when he came three years ago. It was in the deepest part of the bamboo forest, where large bamboos stood and the bamboo was harder.

In the morning, a faint mist wafted through the forest, like a gauze, and the green bamboo leaves on both sides of the trail were crystal clear with dewdrops.

After walking for a while, I was exposed to the green sea. The black bamboos here were mostly towering, with lush foliage, straight into the sky, and the light penetrated through the gap between the branches and leaves, leaving a shadow on the ground. Zhang Xiaofan looked left and right, picked a large black knot bamboo, compared with a picture, then raised his knife to cut.

“Poof” Suddenly there was a muffled noise. Zhang Xiaofan only felt a pain in his forehead, but was hit by his forehead with something. He looked down, and a pine cone rolled on the ground. He thought for a moment, a smile appeared on the corner of his mouth, and looked around, shouting, “Sister, are you?”

His voice spread far away in the bamboo forest, but no one answered for a while. Zhang Xiaofan knew that Sister Sister had always been naughty and teasing, and was about to shout again. Suddenly, there was a pain in his forehead. The pain was so severe that he was thrown into a pine cone again, and there was a “squeak” above his head. Screams.

Zhang Xiaofan looked up with pain and saw that on this black bamboo, I don’t know when to crawl a gray-haired monkey, holding a few pine cones in his hand, and his tail was hanging on the bamboo branch, “squeaky” laughed sharply It’s a great fortune to be happy.

Zhang Xiaofan stayed for a while. He has never seen a monkey in a bamboo forest in the past three years, and there are almost all bamboo forests on the peak of the bamboo. Zhang Xiaofan saw that he couldn’t help the monkey. He was even more annoyed and pulled out a hatchet to cut the bamboo. The monkey was not afraid, and only looked at him with interest on the bamboo.

Zhang Xiaofan’s head was covered with sweat, and finally he scored seven or eight points. Seeing the success was imminent, he suddenly heard a scream from the bamboo, looked up, and saw that the gray monkey’s tail swayed, his body flew up, and actually jumped to the side On another black bamboo, then with a “snap”, another pine cone was thrown down.

Zhang Xiaofan was full of anger, hated going down the mountain, and ignored the monkey. Unexpectedly, the monkey was addicted to play. Tian Linger’s red lips flattened, he couldn’t help laughing, and two small dimples appeared beside his face. Zhang Xiaofan seemed to be teased by her, but it seemed like something else. His face was inexplicably hot, and he looked down.

Tian Ling’er figured out the direction of his escape from the front, back, left, and right, but he didn’t expect the gray monkey to fall. He couldn’t help but stunned and grabbed an empty space.

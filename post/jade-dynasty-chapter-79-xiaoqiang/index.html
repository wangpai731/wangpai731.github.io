He flew out and landed in front of the Yuqing hall. A moment later, a bloodstain slowly flowed from the corner of his mouth, but he was sneering.

On top of his right hand, he held a short sword horizontally. It was as crystal as water. At a glance, he knew that it was not ordinary. At the moment, there are many bloodstains on the body of the sword, and the bright red blood trickles down from the blade to the green bricks on the hall.

All of a sudden, there was a dead silence among the confused crowd just now.

The dark green Taoist robe of immortal daoxuan has turned dark in the twinkling of an eye at the abdomen, and his whole face suddenly turns pale. However, the color of amazement on his face at the moment is far better than the pain on his body.

“You, what are you doing?” In his hoarse voice, he asked all the questions to the Taoist Cangsong standing at the gate of the hall. At this moment, even Qi Hao and Lin Jingyu, the disciples of longshoufeng, could hardly believe their eyes. He pointed to immortal daoxuan, and then pointed to the Sanqing God in the shadow behind the crowd, saying loudly: “you, you,” he pointed to the first Qingyun seat, such as Zhitian not easy, Shuiyue, Zeng Shuchang, Shang Zhengliang, etc. “you all said to me conscientiously, who should be the leader?

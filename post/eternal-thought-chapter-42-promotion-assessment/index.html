When there is no promotion assessment in this area, almost no one comes here. Only when there is a promotion assessment, can there be a large number of visitors.

In addition to the students to be assessed, most of them are friends of these people, or those who want to participate but feel uncertain. They hope to observe more times and sum up experience.

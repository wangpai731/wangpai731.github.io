There was silence all around, as if the only sound echoed in my ears was my own fierce heartbeat, and the fear of the unknown death was like endless cold water, drowning the wild dog.

The wild dog’s breathing slowly calmed down. He was a little strange, but still afraid. He slowly opened his eyes and saw this place. I don’t know when to start, but there were only two people including him.

Until the man turned around and looked at him.

In front of them, there is still endless darkness.

“He’s gone.” The figure is light and authentic.

The old man’s mouth moved, slowly stood up, turned his head and looked at the man, the supreme leader in Qingyun sect.

“Thank you very much.” He said all of a sudden.

After a moment’s silence, immortal daoxuan also slowly walked into the ancestral hall. In the middle of the hall, in front of the platform where countless ancestral places of Qingyun were enshrined, the lights and candles were burning, and the faces of the people illuminated were dark.

Before the old man came to the altar, he picked up a brand-new candle from the incense table in front of the altar, lit it on the other candle, and replaced the next one that was about to burn out.

Immortal daoxuan silently watched his slow and skillful movements, and suddenly said, “I took a big risk and saved you without telling your teachers, but you didn’t even thank me. Why do you thank me for being a teenager today? "

The old man did not speak. He stepped back and was in the shadow, holding the candle he had just replaced. He raised his head and looked up. The ancestral throne of Qingyun gate stood in front of him. It was holy and solemn, like a mountain, as if he was easily overwhelmed by a small man.

The palm of the hand seemed to tremble.

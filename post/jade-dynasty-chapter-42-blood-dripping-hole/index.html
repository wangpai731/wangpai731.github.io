Zhang Xiaofan didn’t expect that Biyao had such a big reaction. She was shocked and pointed to the top of the cave and said, “there are some red stones…”

Biyao immediately approached and looked at the top of the cave. Sure enough, through the water drop, there were seven and a half palm sized red stones inlaid in the top of the cave. The texture of the stone was the same as the stone beside, except for the color.

Zhang Xiaofan saw that the face of Bagao was tense, and he was absorbed in looking at the stone wall on the top of the cave. He was also curious. He stood up and looked there. However, once away from the red stones, the water drops will return to their original transparent appearance.

He looked at it, and suddenly heard the words in the mouth of Bagao beside him: “blood dripping hole, blood dripping hole, blood dripping Ha! " All of a sudden, Bagao was very happy. Zhang Xiaofan’s face suddenly turned white when she clapped Zhang Xiaofan with her right hand. The power of this palm was very strong.

Zhang Xiaofan was furious and was about to ask, but he saw the woman smiling at her face. He didn’t care. No wonder we haven’t found it dozens of times in 800 years.” Zhang Xiaofan was surprised, but he thought about it in his mind. However, most of the secret weapons were collected in a secret place called “blood dripping hole” under the “wanbat ancient cave”, the foundation of the blood refining hall.

Now, in the past 800 years, countless things that predecessors could not do were in front of her. How unhappy she was, for a moment she completely forgot that she was in the Jedi.

Biyao was so happy that she stared at the top of the cave. Then she got up and reached out carefully to touch the red stones, but her hands were cold. But it is no different from the stone beside. She knocked the red stones gently again, but there was no response. At this time, her face was more nervous than excited.

She then pulled, knocked, lifted, smashed and pulled. All the techniques were used, and every red stone was touched. But everything was as usual, and nothing unusual happened.

Zhang Xiaofan looked down, a burst of joy in his heart, couldn’t help laughing: “I don’t think this is a blood hole at all, you guessed it wrong!” Biyao has no choice but to fall to the ground and glare at Zhang Xiaofan, but she has doubts in her heart. Is it really her mistake?

For the next two hours, Zhang Xiaofan sat on the ground and watched the young girl in the water green clothes, frowning, pacing back and forth, pondering hard and using the new methods to deal with the red stones from time to time, but none of them failed.

Looking at it, Zhang Xiaofan suddenly felt that he was hungry when he cried twice.

He reached into his arms and wanted to take some dry food with him to satisfy his hunger. Unexpectedly, it was empty. Most of it was accidentally lost in the water just now. At this time, I was crying bitterly. At present, I was hungry. There was nothing to eat in the cave. The water in the small pool in front of me was very clear, but it was so clear that I didn’t even see a small fish or shrimp.

Seeing that the hunger in his stomach was becoming more and more serious and more and more painful, Zhang Xiaofan had nothing to do but to take a sip of clear water and drink it, but he was totally helpless.

He sighed miserably. It seemed that he was going to starve to death here.

At this time, Biyao was not hungry at all. Her whole mind was on the seven red stones, but after a long time of busy, she finally got nothing and sat down, but her eyes were still staring at the red stones, stunned.

Zhang Xiaofan looked at her like that and couldn’t help reminding her, “what’s the use of that? We don’t want to go out any more. We’re afraid we’ll starve here first.” Biyao’s body moved for a moment. It seemed that she remembered that there was a little disciple in the middle of the way around her. If we find out where the hole is, there will be another way out Zhang Xiaofan thought about it, but there was some truth in it. He wanted to live. The seven red stones arranged like spoons are just in the top of the cave.

Except for the red color, it was totally the same as the surrounding stones. Zhang Xiaofan watched for a while, but he got nothing. He wanted to go up and knock one by one, but he gave up when he thought that there was no way for Biyao to try.

The two men looked up at each other from the beginning, and then they sat on the ground tired. Later, Zhang Xiaofan did not care about any etiquette at all, so did the dying man. Actually lying on the ground, looking at the top of the cave, I don’t know how long it took, but I still didn’t find anything. In the end, I fell asleep.

It took Zhang Xiaofan a long time to wake up and look at it, but she still stared at the seven red stones.

Zhang Xiaofan’s heart at the moment also had some admiration for this woman and sat up. Unexpectedly, the body just moved, and suddenly the stomach was “cooing” again. It seemed that he was very hungry and didn’t give the host face at all.

There was no other sound in the cave except for the sound of water dripping. The sound of “Gugu” rang here. It was introduced into the ears of Baguio at that time and turned to look over.

Zhang Xiaofan is very embarrassed. He can die, but he can’t lose his face. He immediately turns his head to the other side and doesn’t go to see Baguio, but he still feels a heat on his face. He goes to the small water pool and wants to hold some water to drink, which helps him.

The clear water in the pool is only afraid of the mountain springs gushing out from the bottom of the ground. Outside the cold, it is clear and refreshing, more like some sweet taste. But after Zhang Xiaofan took two drinks, the hunger in his stomach was even stronger.

After all, water can’t be used as food. Zhang Xiaofan sighed and stared at the water, but saw the water drop from the top of the cave and into the water, making ripples and floating out in circles. Under the water, his haggard face is reflected, slightly red

He saw some red spots on his reflection face in the water, but then he found that they were wrong. After a careful look, he looked up to the stone wall. It was the red stones on the top of the cave that reflected in the water and overlapped with his own reflection that made this situation.

Zhang Xiaofan breathed a sigh of relief, but at this time, he moved in his heart, stepped back and gazed at the water, but saw the ripples of the water, and finally gradually emerged the reflection of seven red stones in the water pool.

Because of the reflection, their arrangement is no longer that strange spoon shape, but a little like a person’s palm.

Zhang Xiaofan stood in place, did not speak for a while, at that moment he suddenly hesitated, just like suddenly feeling that he stood at an intersection, do not know where to go?

The feeling flashed, and he turned around and said, “Hello!” Biyao is still looking at the stone wall above her head, and casually says, “I don’t call you hello, that’s what you said when you met me at the beginning.” Zhang Xiaofan’s words were almost poured back to his mouth, but somehow he had extraordinary endurance towards the light woman and said, “what’s your name?” “My name is Baguio,” said Baguio, turning to smile

Zhang Xiaofan pointed to the surface of the water, and Biyao leaned over to see that the water was rippling, but after concentration, he slowly saw the reflection of the seven red stones in the water like the palm of his hand.

Clear water from her black hair. Slide down, slowly flow through her white skin, as if even her face is pretty almost transparent.

Zhang Xiaofan suddenly looked crazy, but felt that the original sound of the water in the cave suddenly went away. In his eyes, there was only this beautiful woman standing in front of him, like a lotus in the water, who came to him with the moving beauty.

At the same time, Biyao and Zhang Xiaofan saw that behind the water curtain, the stone wall, which was once seamless and hard, had retreated back completely. Although it was slow, a new hole finally appeared.

Zhang Xiaofan stared at the opening of the secret cave. He was a little excited and scared. But in his deep heart, he felt as if he didn’t want to admit it. He was still curious.

What will be in the important land of the demon cult in the past 800 years?

Biyao slowly walked onto the bank and stood beside him. Her eyes were flowing and full of laughter. Zhang Xiaofan looked at her, but saw her skin as snow and beautiful as ever. There were more crystal water drops on her face, falling down, almost like beating in the heart.

Zhang Xiaofan and Biyao are very careful. After all, this is the first time that someone has come here in 800 years. No one knows whether the old monsters of the blood refining hall in those years will leave some particularly powerful prohibitions.

The ghost King stared at the tripod shaped inscription in his hand. After a moment, he began to laugh slowly. The laughter was full of violence, with a bit of madness and even hysteria

“Success, success, finally success! “Ah,” he gasped deeply. His voice had already become hoarse. His facial muscles were twisted and his face was as red as blood.

Suddenly, he suddenly clenched his right hand into a fist. In an instant, a powerful force came from his body. He could almost see the tiny little blood awn beating like a small lightning in his hand, and the surrounding stone walls seemed to have been pulled by a huge force. In a moment, a large piece of rock fell down and the rocks were flying.

“Ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha”

In the roaring laughter, with madness and pride, the ghost King walked toward the entrance of the stone chamber. But at the entrance of the stone chamber, he suddenly stopped for a moment. In the craziness and violence on his face, he suddenly showed a hesitation and moved his body, as if he wanted to turn around and have a look.

Behind him is the quiet body of Biyao.

However, after a short moment of hesitation, the ghost king did not turn back. The huge and hard stone gate suddenly split and flew out in front of him. In the fierce earthquake and the splash of stone rain, the ghost King strode out of the cold stone room with a fierce determination.

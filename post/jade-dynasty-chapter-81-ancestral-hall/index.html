Jade dynasty--Chapter 81 ancestral hall

2020-06-20
fantasy

Contents

There are two important places in the back mountain of Qingyun Mountain. One is the most important holy land of Qingyun gate, the magic moon cave. The amazing founder of Qingyun gate thousands of years ago closed his door and became the leader of the world.

After the founder of Qingye, the magic moon cave became the most sacred place of Qingyun sect. For thousands of years, only the leader could enter here.

The other place is the ancestral hall.

As the name suggests, it is the place where the ancestors of Qingyun gate are worshipped. From Qingyun son who founded Qingyun gate to Qingye ancestor to the ancestors of all generations, there are holy places in the ancestral hall of Qingyun gate. They are full of fragrance every day. And every important day, under the leadership of the headmaster, Qingyun sect will ceremoniously worship the ancestors here, which is also an important part of Qingyun sect.

But in addition to the day of ancestor worship, it is very cold here. When Lin Jingyu led the four disciples of the demon sect into this place, he saw a magnificent hall, with four cornices, glazed tile roofs, and red pillars of antique doorplates, all of which seemed to tell the history of the past in this quiet place.

From the outside, we can see that there are a lot of candles in the hall, and the long light is shaking and hanging in the air. However, except for an old man in plain clothes who sweeps the floor in silence in front of the palace, no one can be seen.

At this time, heard the rapid footsteps, the old man slowly raised his head, looking here.

Lin Jingyu regretted for a while. At that time, he just wanted to lead the people of the demon sect away from the magic moon cave, but he mistakenly went to the ancestral hall, where the ancestral throne was enshrined. If it was destroyed by the people of the demon sect, he could not be blamed!

As soon as he thought about this, Lin Jingyu stopped, cut the dragon sword in front of him, and turned to face the fake Shangguan policy and others.

At this time, the distant woods suddenly have birds to start, a burst of noise.

Lin Jingyu was shocked. Looking at that direction is where Zhang Xiaofan is, I can’t help worrying. But after all, I know that there is a big enemy in front of me. I have made up my mind secretly. Even if I die here today, I will never let these evil sect thieves step into the ancestral hall.

The Dragon cutting sword seems to have aura. The green light is shining against its master’s face. Perseverance is the best.

The old man sweeping the floor in front of the ancestral hall looked at the Dragon cutting sword in Lin Jingyu’s hand, and suddenly his body was shocked.

The four people of the demon sect over there all smiled at each other. Obviously, these people are not inferior in the demon sect. At a glance, they can see that this place must be the important place of Qingyun sect. It seems that this time there will be a great harvest.

The fake official said with a smile, “little guy, I think you have good qualifications. Now Qingyun gate has no way to go. Why don’t you join us? I promise you will be prosperous in the future! "

“Bah! “Lin Jingyu was disgusted. A sneer, no reason.

The fake official did not get angry. He he sneered and said, “well, since you are going to die, I will complete you! “Then, with a wink, the other three men in black attacked.

Lin Jingyu clenched his teeth and cut the dragon sword, which was rippling in front of him. The magic weapon used by the three men in black is a yellow flying sword and a powerful long halberd. Another is the most strange and terrifying. It’s a white bone sword made of several bones. It’s full of Yin Qi.

Lin Jingyu fought hard with one enemy and three. The Dragon cutting sword is green and the guards are in the same place, but gradually they are still in the downwind.

Lin Jingyu’s talent is excellent when he enters Qingyun gate. Taoist Cangsong attaches great importance to him. Not only did he teach him carefully, but even the Dragon cutting sword with great potential was handed to him. I don’t know if he saw the shadow of the man he admired in the past.

And Lin Jingyu did not fail to live up to the painstaking efforts of Taoist Cangsong. In just a few years, with his excellent talent and the deep-rooted belief in revenge for his parents and relatives, Taoism has made rapid progress, and has been the leader of the younger generation of disciples for several years.

He was only allowed to work hard again, but his practice was ultimately limited by time and could not be too radical. At the moment, facing the three disciples of the evil cult in black, the halberd in the front kept falling, while the flying sword next to him was aiming at the air to sneak in; what’s more, the white bone sword, with the cold Yin Qi, suddenly appeared under the control of the evil cult man. Every time he blocked it, he could not help but take a breath of cool Qi and shiver.

In such a bitter fight for dozens of rounds, even though Lin Jingyu defended with all his strength, the green light of the dragon sword was gradually overwhelmed by the three men in black. Seeing his defeat, he finally stepped back.

The fake superior officer standing behind made a sneer.

The three men in black were in high spirits. All the magic weapons were out, and Lin Jingyu was sweating. He stepped back one after another. He wanted to stand but could not.

Suddenly, the mysterious white bone sword suddenly disappeared. Lin Jingyu was holding back the long halberd that he had smashed. Then he swung the flying sword that was attacking from the side. Unexpectedly, he felt a pain at his feet and couldn’t stand when he stepped on it. It was the white bone sword that sneaked into the ground at some time. When it came, it cut a big hole in his right leg. It was bloody.

Lin Jingyu roared, and the dragon sword fell into the sky. The immortal family’s immortal soldier hit the white bone sword, and it crackled slightly. The owner of the white bone sword fell out immediately, and there was a slight crack on the sword.

The man of the demon sect who used the white bone sword suffered a lot. He quickly took the white bone sword back and looked at it carefully. But at this time, the magic weapon of the other two people has arrived. The wind is cold. Lin Jingyu is in the death place. He uses his last strength to control and cut the Dragon Sword across his head.

With a loud bang, I saw the strange light flickering and sparks shooting all around. I didn’t know where the power came from. The Dragon chopping Jiansheng stopped the two magic weapons, but Lin Jingyu was also in the dark. In this moment of lightning and stone fire, he lost his mind for a moment, and suddenly saw the fake shangce standing in the distance appeared in front of him, grinning at himself.

Lin Jingyu lost his color in a panic, but before he could react, he felt a deep pain in his chest, and a sharp force came in, piercing all his body protection methods instantly.

“Ah! "

Lin Jingyu roared, and the whole man flew out, spouting blood. He couldn’t hold the dragon sword. The body of the sword rolled in the air. The last brush fell into the ground, just in front of the old man sweeping the floor.

The green light flows, and gradually becomes dim.

Lin Jingyu has always been stubborn. He looks down at his chest, but he sees a piece of flesh and blood. The real wound is the size of a fingertip. But at the moment, a rush of energy rushed into the body, which was like an impregnable fine needle. The internal meridians were so painful that they wanted to stand up and meet the enemy again. Unexpectedly, the foot of a soft, it is unable to stand up!

He gasped for breath, but the fake shangguance and others laughed and were extremely proud.

Lin Jingyu’s muscles twitched on his face. Obviously, his body was in great pain. But he was extremely worried by the impending difficulties. When he thought of the ancestral hall behind him, he didn’t know where his strength came from. He struggled to get up slowly.

The devil cult and others didn’t stop him, just watching monkey opera.

The pain in the cone’s heart seemed to rush to the top of his head. Before Lin Jingyu could stand still, he was dizzy again. The old man looks like the person who cleans the ancestral hall in Qingyun gate. His face is haggard and wrinkled like a knife. It’s strange to say that Lin Jingyu fought with all the people of the demon sect, but he also stood quietly and watched, neither escaping. He didn’t talk.

The smile of the four people of the demon sect was smothering, and the appearance of the official policy was suddenly cold, saying, “I can’t imagine there are still senior people here. pretty good. I’m Zhou Yin. Who is your excellency? "

The old man sighed and said, “who am I? Hey, even I forget who I am. “When he spoke, his face was sorrowful and his tone was full of desolation. Then he turned to Lin Jingyu, who was stunned,” son. "

The old man nodded and whispered, “it’s Cangsong! He has a good eye for his apprentice. "

Looking at his slow movement, Lin Jingyu suddenly felt nervous, as if he was expecting something from his heart. He could not help but hold his breath.

That pair of withered and old palms, don’t know how much wind and frost he has experienced, when he once again touched the hard and cold hilt, then, clenched it!

The old man shook his head slightly as if he was sighing something. Then he looked at the dragon sword in his hand and threw it to Lin Jingyu.

Lin Jingyu catches him subconsciously, only to hear the old man return to the ancestral hall, and slowly say, “cut the dragon sword, take the green crystal from the extremely bitter south of Xinjiang, and kill countless evils.

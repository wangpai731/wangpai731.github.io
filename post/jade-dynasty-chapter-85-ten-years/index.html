Black clouds hover in the night sky. The sky is dark as if it is pressing against the ground. The rain falling from the sky rolls over the vast land in the wind.

In the wilderness, there is no village before and no village behind. There is only one ancient road extending from afar, and it extends to the distance alone.

In the dark clouds of the night sky, there was a low thunder sound, and the rain between the heaven and the earth gradually increased.

The land is solemn. In addition to the wind and rain, it’s dark all around. Only on the edge of the ancient road, alone, a little light is lit, revealing a little light.

This is a wild cottage. The owner’s surname is he. He is a middle-aged man in his early forties. He was born in Xiaochi Town, a day’s journey from here. In this remote place, beside the ancient road, he built a simple house to provide a place for tourists from south to north to rest and drink tea, so as to earn some hard money.

At the moment, boss he is sitting behind the counter in his shop, listening to the shrill wind and rain outside the house, frowning slightly and sighing softly. In such bad weather, most of the guests will not come again.

His cottage is located between Xiaochi town and kongsang mountain, a big mountain in the West. The ancient road outside the gate has been the road of business and travel since ancient times, and it is also the necessary way to the big city in the East, donghaichang. Therefore, tourists pass by from time to time.

At present, on this stormy night, there are still several guests in this wild shop, sitting silently in the dark and yellow hut, avoiding the miserable wind and rain outside.

“Crackle.”

He thought so, looking up at the guests in his shop. There are only five tables in the simple hut. At present, there are three tables beside the guests. The old man was stunned, but he didn’t speak. In front of him was the table of the young lady and the young lady. His eyes also looked at them. The young lady had bright eyes, beautiful looks, and shallow dimples at the corners of her mouth, which made her attractive.

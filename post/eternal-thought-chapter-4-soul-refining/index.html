“The fire is not ordinary. It’s not only burning fast, but also much hotter than the fire in the village.” Bai Xiaochun looked at the wood in the fire stove again and thought that it should be a good wood.

At this time, with the flame burning, Bai Xiaochun was surprised to see that the first line on the turtle shaped pot turned bright from bottom to top. Soon, all the lines were lit up.

Almost as soon as he practiced, Bai Xiaochun’s eyes widened. He found that the difficult posture he had put out yesterday was extremely smooth, and he didn’t feel uncomfortable at the moment. Even the way of breathing, there was no suffocation, but a very comfortable feeling.

In particular, he clearly remembered that before the most is three or four breaths, but now seven or eight breaths have passed without any pain.

Endure the exhilaration, Bai Xiaochun calmed himself down until he held on to thirty breaths. When he felt weak pain in his body, suddenly, a wisp of air appeared from his body. With experience, Bai Xiaochun quickly followed the signs on the first picture and silently thought about several routes in his body.

Soon, the air trickle in his body followed the route as Bai Xiaochun thought. With Bai Xiaochun still sticking to the action of putting out the first picture, he even noticed that there was a trace of cool air coming out from all parts of his body, like water drops, and integrated into the trickle of Qi, making the trickle bigger and bigger.

An unprecedented feeling of lightness immediately appeared on his body, and a mass of dirt was continuously secreted along his sweat pores.

Even the body obviously feels lighter.

“Qi pulse is always present, which is the performance of the first level of Ziqi controlling tripod skill, and also represents the first level of condensing gas.” I can’t help but go out in vain.

After seeing this, Zhang daphou and others showed a smile that they knew each other. Although they were surprised that Bai Xiaochun had completed the first layer so quickly, they understood why.

Returning to the house, Bai Xiaochun took a few deep breaths, picked up the bamboo book on one side and looked at it carefully.

“After the first level of controlling tripod skill, you can control some objects. This is the magic power of immortals. You can take pictures from the sky.” According to the above method, Bai Xiaochun pinched out a simple seal with both hands and pointed to the table beside him. At once, he felt the stream in his body, like a runaway wild horse running straight to his right index finger, even out of the fingertip.

It seems that an invisible silk thread is formed and connected with the table. Unfortunately, as soon as it is connected, the thread is unstable and breaks with a crack.

Bai Xiaochun was pale, and it took a long time to recover. After careful consideration, he gave up the table and took out the wooden sword from his pocket. He did not know what wood the wooden sword was made of. Although it was not as heavy as the table, it was also a little heavy. He raised a finger with his right hand.

The wooden sword suddenly vibrated and floated slowly, but it only rose an inch and then fell down again.

Bai Xiaochun was not discouraged. After many exciting attempts, the wooden sword dropped from the beginning to ten, twenty and thirty inches At dusk, the wooden sword in his room could float away in a straight line. Although it was not fast and difficult to turn, it would not fall as easily as it did in the early days.

“From then on, I am a fairy Bai Xiaochun stood there, a proud look, with his left hand behind him, his right hand raised and waved forward, and the wooden sword swayed back and forth.

When the breath in his body was unstable, Bai Xiaochun took back his wooden sword and was about to continue his practice. Suddenly, he smelled the aroma coming from the pot on the side. He looked up and took a deep breath, and immediately his appetite moved. On this day, he was busy practicing, but he forgot that there was still LingMi in the pot, so he went forward to open the lid of the pot.

At the moment of opening the lid, a strong aroma came out from the LingMi in the pot, but on the LingMi, I don’t know why, there was a sharp silver grain!

“It’s a little familiar…” White small pure eyes show thinking, look down at the fire stove, found that the fire inside has long been extinguished, even wood has become ashes, and a bright line on the pot, also dim again.

As like as two peas, he recognized the grain of the rice on the grain.

In order to be safe, Bai Xiaochun did not eat the rice, but put it in a cloth bag. After thinking for a moment, she walked out of the house and worked with Zhang daphou and others.

As time went by, half a month passed by in a flash. In the past half month, Bai Xiaochun’s practice stopped again and made slow progress. However, he also found out that when others cooked LingMi, there would be no silver grain.

Curious, he felt more and more that his grain of rice was not right, especially the pot.

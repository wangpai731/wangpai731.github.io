The darkness is boundless, only the sound of the wind is more and more bleak. The Lich walked in the dark corridor of the ancient cave, just like a ghost walking towards the nine secluded places.

The older the cave is, the wider it becomes, but the darkness around it grows deeper. Walking on this cold and terrible Road, the Lich can even close its eyes and walk forward.

For many years, he wandered alone here, and now, he finally wants to change his own destiny.

Perhaps, and the fate of countless people in the world.

The wind roars in front of him!

A faint light suddenly lit up in front of him. Though the light was so dim, it was particularly striking in the darkness.

The Lich stopped.

The faint light flickered in the dark, and it was like a call, a temptation, a desire, a sneer

The wind, blowing his black skirt, like the past countless years, he gazed at the place.

Many years ago, he also stood here, but at that time, there was a brother beside him, and in front of him, there was a figure that was thin but seemed to block the world.

But now, he is the only figure alone.

“Empress…” He lowered his head slightly and called out in a low voice.

Then he floated forward, casting a light like a moth.

The dark light is great, and the wind in the ancient cave is suddenly fierce. Originally only a little light, slowly scattered from that place, will slowly light up around.

On the uneven ground, there are decayed bones everywhere, some of them are figures and some are beasts. The huge wall of the cave, hard rock, in the light, but showed countless dense, crisscross cracks, as if torn apart by life, shocking.

In the dark, there’s a voice. In the deepest part of the dim light, with a chill, gently reverberates.

“You’re back…”

The ending sound is very long, reverberating between the walls of this ancient cave.

The Lich did not speak. He just stood in the light, stood for a moment, and then, out of the black, put his arm on his hand. It is a black stick inlaid with bone jade.

“Roar!…”

A roar, suddenly like thunder, boiling in the ancient cave. The darkness around him retreated in an instant, and in the depths of that dark awn, a dazzling light flashed in a twinkling, like the innumerable tentacles of demons, shouting and Howling towards the Lich and the two sacred vessels.

Even the stone wall of the ancient cave around it has been shaking for thousands of years. Big rocks and small stones fall.

The wind, whistling and shrill, at the moment, sounds like a long, heavy breath.

“…… Do you remember what a lady looks like The Lich looked at the dazzling light in front of him, and suddenly said so quietly.

In the strong light, the twinkling light suddenly solidified.

The Lich is dressed in black, hunting in the strong wind.

Even his voice sounded so erratic: “her stone statue is still standing on the hole outside…”

Deep in the light, there was no sound. Only the stretching and shrinking light makes the Lich’s figure bright and dark.

The Lich did not say any more, and slowly floated up into the depths of the light.

A flat, open place. It is quite different from the outside. The hard stone walls are mostly intact, but on the ground, there are many huge bones, and most of them are in good condition. There are as many as thirteen.

These 13 skeletons with different shapes and emitting the spirit of evil spirits are all wrapped in a circle at different distances, all of them are inside the face, back and outside, as if guarding something. In the dark and empty eye hole, it seems that there are cold eyes.

As the figure of the Lich suddenly appeared, it began to approach this strange circle. Suddenly, a sour “Chueh” sound appeared in the cold wind. On these bones, there were several heads that started to turn, slowly turned around and looked in the direction of the Lich.

In this terrible time when the heart almost stopped beating, the Lich did not seem to care about these terrible skeletons. His eyes, from the beginning to the end, were only looking at one place.

That’s the middle of the circle of thirteen white bones.

A life size white bone was quietly lying on a white jade platform only three inches high. Unlike the skeletons around it, the human skeleton was also covered with silk. I don’t know how many years it has gone through. Under the light, the color of the silk is still incomparably bright.

And all the light around, even the howling wind, are from the white bone.

The Lich drifted closer to the white bone.

Light flow, strange light for a short time, as if in the dark, eyes are watching him.

Around, all the 13 white bones suddenly gave out the sound of “Kuai”, almost as if they were resurrected together. Their heads turned and their deep eye holes were staring at the figure of the Lich.

The next moment, that piece of silk rises in the air, floating in the air.

It was as if there was a silent low roar. In an instant, the dazzling light shone from under the silk, like an unstoppable arrow, roaring away in all directions.

“Woo!” The Lich even felt that the light with surging Demon power rushed through his ears.

The fierce wind, mixed with a gloomy sneer, began to reverberate in this ancient cave.

All of a sudden, the thirteen skeletons looked up and roared to the sky!

Under this strange atmosphere, the Lich slowly fell in front of the bones. There are five bone fragments in his spine and right hand, which are very clear in the upper part of his spine and his right hand.

At the moment, reflecting the light of his bones, he had a white bead on his right hand, a jade dish on his left ankle, and a ring at the broken throat.

The Lich slowly pulled out the bone jade inlaid on the black stick, and then gently put it on the white bone’s head. There was a small hole in the middle of the forehead, and the bone jade just put it in.

In the dark, it seems that there is a voice, calling from afar.

The Lich’s body suddenly trembled, and the whole person shook. The light reflected in his eyes, like two burning white flames.

Whose soul and body is the flame burning?

The howling wind stopped, the dazzling white light disappeared, the darkness like the boundless sea, the surging waves silently rushed up and submerged everything!

Who is waiting in the dark?

The deepest darkness. Or the dawn of fantasy?

Everything calmed down, just like the desolate silence that has never changed since ancient times. The white bones stopped shouting and became silent.

A voice, in the depths of darkness and silence, quietly, rings out!

“Bang!”

“Bang!”

“Bang bang!”

……

It was the sound of heartbeat, full of new vitality, and the surrounding was still dark, but the heartbeat sound like magic was gradually amplified. Slowly, began to flow gurgling water sound.

No, it’s not the sound of the water, it’s the blood flowing from the heart, dancing wildly in the dark with endless joy and irresistible momentum.

Long sleep for countless years, after endless cold, once again warm!

Who is breathing quietly in the dark?

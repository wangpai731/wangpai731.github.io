Lu Xueqi was silent, and suddenly there was pain on her face. She said, “master, it shouldn’t have been like this. It wouldn’t have been like this!”

The water moon seemed to be silent, and after a while she said softly, “it’s all life, Qi’er. When you see him again in the future, you will be the enemy of the world, and you should remember clearly. "

Then she sighed and turned away.

Only Lu Xueqi was left standing on the spot alone. The mountain wind blew. She felt a chill on her body. She looked at the distance in silence and said in a low voice: “when I meet him again…”

The night is as cold as water, shining on her lonely figure. (to be continued. )

Looking at the small wooden box, the whole box was plain and without any decoration. Even the wood used was the most common tree in southern Xinjiang. The great wizard, like countless Miao people, was quietly sleeping here.

He bent his body and bowed deeply.

With a squeak, the monkey jumped from him and ran to one side.

He nodded his head in silence and looked at the God of the dog in the distance. He said slowly, “the great wizard is also dead for me. Every time I think about it, I feel uneasy.”

Blood sucking pearl Demon power has puzzled him for many years, but a while ago, under the wordless jade wall of Tianyin temple in Xumishan mountain, he realized the four volumes of the book of heaven. For him, with the improvement of his cultivation, the power of blood sucking pearl demon is no longer a major obstacle to the relationship between nature and life 。

After a careful look, all the evil spirits in the huge array are still as clear and vivid as those in the memory. The patterns connecting these evil spirits are also weird and complex.

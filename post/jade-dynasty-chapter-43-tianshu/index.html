I saw Biyao stand up and take a look at Zhang Xiaofan. He looked elsewhere and stood still. His brow frowned, but he didn’t say anything. He said lightly, “let’s go!”

Zhang Xiaofan had some good feelings for the woman when he came in the tunnel, but when he saw these two evil gods, he thought of the difference between the sects and the teachings of his teacher since he was a child. Naturally, he was cold, nodded slightly, and said, “OK.”

After seeing him, Biyao went deeper in. Zhang Xiaofan followed her. This time, she didn’t go far. She went into a spacious place. But it’s not like the stone room outside. It’s a grotesque cave with all kinds of stalactites hanging upside down. In front of the two people, there is a huge stele standing at the entrance. On the top of it, there are ten words: Heaven and earth are not benevolent, and everything is the ruminant dog!

Each of these ten characters is almost half the size of a person. It has an ancient and unsophisticated brushwork and vigorous brushwork. It goes straight to the dragon and snake, but it comes out face-to-face and roars in the sky.

Zhang Xiaofan didn’t see much at first, but after looking at it for a moment, he suddenly felt that his head was in a daze, and the whole person could not help but step back.

He was startled and quickly calmed down, but saw that the word was still on the monument, motionless, but it was really scary.

Zhang Xiaofan was surprised. In a second, she saw that Biyao had bypassed the monument and walked deep into the cave. Then she followed. Around the stone tablet, I saw that behind it, there were all kinds of strange stalactites. They walked around in the stone forest for a while, and Biyao who was walking in front of them suddenly stopped and cried softly.

And almost at the same time, Zhang Xiaofan suddenly found that the firebrand he used to fix his arm suddenly flashed a strange light, especially the bead in the front of the firebrand. But this time it was soft. It was like meeting old friends and acquaintances that we haven’t seen for many years. With endless thoughts and thoughts.

Zhang Xiaofan looked forward in surprise, and looked through the side of Biyao. He saw the scene that surprised Biyao in front of him: the bottom of the cave is a smooth stone wall, and there is a tunnel on both sides of the stone wall leading to the unknown place. But under the stone wall is a bluestone platform, on which there is a skeleton, sitting in the shape of an end, sitting there quietly.

And the bead on the fire stick, at the moment, glowed a soft blue light on the skeleton.

Biyao stood in front of him and didn’t notice Zhang Xiaofan’s strange expression and the change of his firestick. After the initial shock, she quickly calmed down.

After all, she is a member of the demon sect. How can you be afraid of a skeleton? Go ahead and look carefully, but you don’t see any accidents. Turn your head to smile at Zhang Xiaofan and say, “maybe this is the black heart old man who was famous eight hundred years ago!”

Zhang Xiaofan naturally had no good feelings for the people in the demon sect. He snorted and said, “let’s find out if there is any way out.”

Biyao took a look at him, sipped her mouth, and said, “you should find it yourself!”

Zhang Xiaofan stayed for a while, some of them could not face it. Hum, unexpectedly turn around and walk into the left tunnel.

Without two steps, he shook his head to himself secretly, and felt that he could not hold his breath in the face of the demon woman. When she was stimulated, she would react so much, for fear that she might be sneering and laughing in her eyes at the moment.

But want to return to think, since stepped out of the footsteps can not turn back, walked a few steps, there is no movement behind. It seems that Biyao didn’t keep up with Zhang Xiaofan. Somehow, Zhang Xiaofan seems to be lost, but then he secretly scolds himself for being “unproductive”. Cheer up and walk carefully to the depth of the tunnel. Zhang Xiaofan’s tunnel is the same as the road when he came from outside, but it’s so deep and quiet. Looking into the deep distance, it’s almost dark, and the road seems to be relatively long. I can’t imagine how those people in the blood refining Hall of the demon cult started such a huge project.

After walking like this for a long time, Zhang Xiaofan suddenly found that the front was gradually brightened. He was three summers in his heart. He stepped forward quickly, only to see the soft light at the end of the road ahead. It was clear in the dark, like a soft touch, tempting people in the world.

Zhang Xiaofan breathed deeply and stepped into the light.

Seeing Zhang Xiaofan’s figure disappear in the tunnel, Biyao was shocked and her face sank. Her father is a high-ranking figure in the demon sect. She has been like a princess since she was a child. No one dares to disobey her.

Unexpectedly, she was in a desperate situation today, but she met a middle-aged man with a good temper. She couldn’t help being angry.

When Zhang Xiaofan was in Qingyun Mountain, he was also a kind young man. Why did he bump with Biyao? Apart from his opinions, he was afraid that he could not make it clear.

These things are unknown to Biyao. However, Zhang Xiaofan’s repeated disagreements with her are really in his eyes, and the eldest one’s discomfort. She turned, almost without thinking, and went into the tunnel on the right.

After a few steps, Biyao felt that it was a road similar to the outer tunnel, but there were fewer things shining on both sides of the stone wall, which made the tunnel a little dark.

Fortunately, this road is not very long, and soon Baguio came to the end and stepped into a stone chamber again.

It’s a medium-sized stone chamber with many shelves on one side and a pile of rubbish on the other. Most of the iron tools, such as knives, swords and guns, are damaged. The obvious purpose is to throw an axe on the top at will. The whole body of iron is quite huge and complete. It looks like it’s made of iron.

After two eyes, Bagao lost interest. She turned to the shelves and looked at them. Her face first showed the color of great joy, but soon she could not help but change into the color of disappointment.

There are labels on the shelves one by one. Some of the words on the shelves have been blurred for a long time, but some of them can barely be seen clearly. However, all of them are names like “five mountain halberd”, “guanyuesuo” and “departing cone”.

Since she was a child, Biyao grew up in the demon sect. Her father is a wizard who knows all the time and has a profound family knowledge. A burst of joy filled Biyao’s heart. She carefully reached out and picked up the box. She felt that her hands were heavy. Gently shake a few times, but no sound.

As soon as Baguio pondered, he put the iron box on the ground, took a deep breath, and was alert. With a wave of his right hand, the white light in the stone chamber suddenly rose, and a jade like flower suddenly appeared in the air, giving off a faint fragrance.

Baguio looked solemn and turned her right hand. The little flower standing in the air is full of light, flying to the top of the iron box, and the white light covers the whole iron box.

Then, Biyao carefully reached out to open the small box. As soon as she pressed the lid of the iron box, Biyao felt that it didn’t seem to be locked. She frowned and her eyes were more alert. She bit the teeth of the shell and opened the lid of the iron box.

Biyao’s face changed greatly, almost turned over like an electric shock, and the white flower on the top of the iron box rushed down immediately, and the black gas was suddenly covered by the white light. Several impulses did not come out. Until the black gas completely dissipated, Bagao also waited for a long time before coming. She looked at her little flower with concentration. Her father devoted great efforts to make her special treasure “sad flower”. At this moment, the petals of white jade had become purple and black, which was somewhat ferocious.

It’s very simple. There is only one thing in a small iron box: a small bell with golden color, intact as a prayer.

After staying for a while, Biyao didn’t expect that there was such a rare and highly poisonous thing as “ancient corpse poison” in the iron box. She just kept such a small bell. She looked left and right, but she didn’t see anything strange. She pondered for a while, and slowly reached out to pick up the small bell.

“Jingle”.

A clear sound, like reverberating in people’s hearts, resounds in the secret stone chamber which has been quiet for 800 years.

Baguio picked up the bell, but saw that the bell was delicate and delicate. A thin iron rope was tied to the bell, and when it was shaken slightly, the bell struck the bell gently, making a sound again.

But black heart old man’s collection is so mysterious and prudent. This bell must have something extraordinary. I’ll ask my father when I have a chance to go out.

Baguio thought so, and settled down. But seeing the little bell, she liked it more and more. She tied it to her waist and turned her body. As expected, there were a series of clear bells, which were very pleasant. Baguio was very proud and nodded.

Then she walked out of the stone chamber.

The tunnel on the left that Zhang Xiaofan just entered is much longer than the road on the right that Biyao entered. Biyao snorted. She wanted to get angry, but when she thought about it, she didn’t say anything. She also looked at the wall. After a few words, she felt dizzy and admired Zhang Xiaofan. He could read such dry and astringent words.

All kinds of miracles and methods in this demon sect are extreme and special, but the root is in the “heavenly book”. Taoism pays attention to body control and nature creation, Buddhism pays attention to self-awareness, but in the book of heaven, it seems to be all-around, including Taoism and Buddhism.

Another person, such as Biyao, naturally has no idea after reading these words. He always thinks it’s a great magic power left by his grandfather. But in the eyes of Zhang Xiaofan, the only one in the world who knows the truth of Tao and Buddhism, it’s a terrible thing.

The bell swallows, the flowers wither,

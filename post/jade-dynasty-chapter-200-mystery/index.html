The ancestral hall of Qingyun Mountain is still shrouded in the pines and cypresses, and the huge figure looms. However, this silence was soon broken by the chaotic steps. A large group of people under the Qingyun gate rushed to this holy land for worshiping the ancestors of Qingyun ancestors.

From the appearance, it seems that everything is still as quiet as before. But before we get to the hall of the ancestral hall, both the confused disciples of Tongtian peak and the anxious Su Ru stop for it.

Surrounded by pines and cypresses, the ancestral hall is solemn and solemn. At the moment, there are broken wood and debris everywhere, which is in a state of chaos.

At the huge gate of the ancestral hall, the original red lacquer gate was smashed. Even the appearance of the door was hard to see. In front of the public, it was just a more huge and piercing grotto.

On the outer wall of the ancestral temple, almost all the windows were shaken down, and countless holes, large or small, appeared on the wall. The solemn ancestral hall was full of holes, which was terrible. Only the darkness in the ancestral hall seemed to ignore the dim light from the falling windows and countless holes, and diffused gently in the ancestral hall.

“Not easy!”

Su Ru was the first to react, but she didn’t care why the ancestral temple had been changed. She rushed in, hoping to see the people she wanted to see. Master Shuiyue and elder Yang, elder fan and others also followed.

In the ancestral hall, it seems that it was also the same as the outside. All the once severe weather conditions were destroyed, the flat stone slabs were broken, and the huge glass oil bottles were also broken. Even when people came to the most sacred place, after the huge sacrificial table which was split into two parts, the countless spiritual tablets of the ancestors of Qingyun gate, which were worshipped, were scattered all over the ground. At a glance, I don’t know how many magic cards have been forcibly beaten into two halves or even more by some mysterious power.

However, in addition to the mess of the whole land, people actually could not see a person.

Su Ru looks pale. The body is crumbling, master Shuiyue frowns tightly, steps on a step and hugs her in his arms. After a few words of consolation in a low voice, he turned to Xiao Yi, who was following the crowd. “What’s going on here? What’s the matter with elder martial brother daoxuan?”

“Someone.” Elder Yang quickly judged that the voice came from the back of the interrupted huge altar.

Su Ru, who was weak in the whole body, suddenly startled. A glimmer of joy flashed in her eyes. She stood up straight and cried, “it’s not easy. Is it you?”

A disciple ran past and opened the table together. I don’t know which generation of ancestors passed it down. It’s huge and thick. It was extremely heavy. Although the disciples had some Taoism, they had to work together to open the table.

After opening. Sure enough, there was a figure under the debris, and a low voice came out at the same time.

The crowd was overjoyed and surrounded, but a moment later, they were stunned again. They saw that this man was not easy. It’s not the Taoist priest of Qingyun sect, but Lin Jingyu, a disciple of longshoufeng who has been guarding the spirit in the ancestral hall.

Half of his body and clothes were red with blood, and he was obviously injured. His face was very pale and seemed to be in a coma. He did not respond to the people kneeling beside him calling him at the moment.

Su Ru’s joy gradually disappeared, and was replaced by greater worry and anxiety. Master Shuiyue stood by her side and comforted with soft voice. Elder Yang looks pale and looks around. The ancestral hall of Qingyun gate is one of the most important places in Qingyun gate, which can be compared with the magic moon cave. It’s really something that has never happened in a thousand years. What’s more, the two most important people in Qingyun gate seem to have disappeared.

“Nephew Xiao,” elder Yang turned to Xiao Yicai and said, “are you sure the leader is here?”

Xiao Yi looked at the unconscious Lin Jingyu. Her face calmed down and pondered for a moment. She said, “yes, since these days, my master is really only in this ancestral hall. It is here that the disciples ask for advice and report back to his old man.”

Elder Yang was obviously upset and didn’t know what to do for a while.

Xiao Yicai coughed twice and approached elder Yang slowly. He lowered his voice and said in a low voice: “Uncle Yang, it’s not appropriate to delay this matter. So many younger martial brothers gather here to see the damage to the Holy Land and ancestral hall, which is harmful and unhelpful. What’s more, according to Su Ru, uncle Su’s teacher is not easy with the field in dazhufeng. He seems to have a secret feeling. I’m afraid it may have something to do with what happened here. Xiao Yicai nodded to elder Yang, which was taken as an order. Then he turned around and said in a loud voice: “martial uncles, younger martial brothers, the ancestral hall has been in great trouble recently. I’m afraid there is an invasion of foreign enemies. He did not hesitate at the moment. After answering in a deep voice, he quickly called on the people and went out. It is obvious that the number of people is not enough. As soon as this large group of people left. At that time, the ancestral hall was obviously empty. There were only a few senior figures and Xiao Yicai, and Wen Min who followed master Shuiyue. Finally, Lin Jingyu was still in a coma.

With a sigh, Xiao Yicai turned and saluted the elders. He said in a low voice, “martial uncles, there are great changes in Qingyun gate today. My disciples are arbitrary in making decisions. If there is something wrong, please punish them.”

Su Ru and master Shuiyue did not speak. Elder Yang nodded and said, “nephew Xiao, you don’t have to be humble. You did a good job just now. Now we don’t have to do anything else. You can just tell me. You’re welcome. "

Xiao Yicai pondered for a moment and said, “now that the situation is not clear, we still need to be careful. Please go back to their respective mountains. If there is a contingency, it is also good for their respective portal vein care. It’s a pity that younger martial brother Lin of longshoufeng is still in a coma. Otherwise, we will know everything by asking him. After all, he was the only one present at that time. "

All of them frowned together. All of them were worried. Su Ru was comforted by master Shuiyue. After all, Tian was not easy and her figure was not there. Although she was worried, there was hope in the end, so she was not so nervous. Listening to Xiao Yicai’s dispatch all the way, she was in a state of confusion, and only hoped that the field would not be in trouble.

At this time, she glanced at Lin Jingyu lying on the ground. Suddenly, she frowned and whispered: “eh!”

Master Shuiyue stood beside her and said in dismay, “what’s the matter?”

Su Ru pointed to Lin Jingyu and said, “what seems to be on his hand?”

Everyone was surprised. Xiao Yi quickly walked to Lin Jingyu and turned him over. Sure enough, he was pressed in his right hand, holding a rectangular black board tightly. Xiao Yicai reaches out to get it. Unexpectedly, as soon as he pulls it out, the board doesn’t move. Although Lin Jingyu is in a coma, he somehow grabs the board tightly and never relaxes.

All of them were puzzled.

Elder fan went to one side and turned around. Suddenly he said, “this board seems to be the spirit card of the ancestor worshipped.”

Master Shuiyue fixed his eyes and nodded: “yes, it’s a magic card.”

Xiao Yi took a long time to break off Lin Jingyu’s tightly held fingers and took out the magic card which seemed to be extremely important to him. Everyone gathered around. As a witness to the incident, Lin Jingyu cared so much about the trump, which obviously had something to do with it. Unexpectedly, all of them were shocked and looked at each other.

Although this trump is the same size as other trumps and is also painted black, there is no word on it.

This is a magic card without words!

Who is the spiritual throne it worships in this solemn ancestral hall?

Who put it to enjoy incense with the ancestors of all ages? Since it was put on it, why didn’t it write its name?

What does it mean for Lin Jingyu to hold on to this wooden card and refuse to let go after being seriously injured and unconscious?

All kinds of doubts, a myriad of thoughts, seem to linger in the hearts of all people.

In response, there was a quiet laugh. “You don’t have to care. It never believed in humans.”

Bright, silk clothes.

Beast God!

He still had a strange feeling of enchantment. However, unlike when he was just born again, his face was extremely pale, and it was not too much to say that his face was as pale as death.

Taotie seemed to be alert at once. He roared and stared at the dark red light.

The light of the fire moved slowly, and the direction was the place where the God of the beast was. Taotie’s face was more ferocious, and he slowly stood up. Suddenly, a hand extended from the side, gently patted Taotie’s head, which slowly quieted down.

The beast God took back his hand and looked back. The light of the fire was slowly flying in front of him. It was like an eye. It was not far away from him. He was staring at him.

The beast God looked at the dark red light for a long time, and suddenly said with a smile, “you and I have been friends for thousands of years. Although we can’t say that we are friends, we are old friends! Besides, I am seriously injured at the moment. How can you be so alert to me? "

The dark red fire flickered for several times, and suddenly gave out a sharp scream, and quickly and incomparably retreated back. At this time, the dark red fire had disappeared in the dark.

Morning bell, evening drum, day after day, as if endless.

Every day is as like as two peas. Yesterday, some people feel boring. Some feel peaceful and quiet.

In a twinkling of an eye, GUI Li has been in Tianyin temple for many days, listening to the bell in the morning and the drum in the evening. He rings from the unknown place in the temple every day on time to live in silence. I don’t know why, after only a few days, he seems to have been integrated into this strange environment. He is silent every day, just in a trance.

He is now in his prime, and his health is excellent. Although he is seriously injured, he is young, and his practice is high. In addition, Tianyin temple is so generous to him that he can use any good medicine. With the reputation of Tianyin temple, the good medicine in the temple is also the first-class good medicine in the world. The efficacy of the medicine is rapid, and his injuries are all good.

However, for a few days, he had been able to get out of bed and walk reluctantly. However, when he walked, he still had a sharp pain in his chest. However, Rao was so, and he also made FA Xiang and others who came to visit him happy. He said that he had never seen a person recover so quickly in the past. It seems that he can recover completely in less than a month.

In ordinary days, GUI Li gets along with them and talks with them occasionally. Both sides avoid talking about their opposite identities. It seems that in the eyes of the Buddhist monks in Faxiang Tianyin temple, GUI Li is just an ordinary person who they treat with good intentions, rather than the evil cult demon who they forcibly snatched from Qingyun gate in spite of the world’s disrespect. And GUI Li no longer asked why people in Tianyin Temple wanted to save him.

As time passed by, his body got better day by day. These days, he was able to walk more easily. Sometimes when the morning bell and evening drum sounded, he would pull a chair to open the window, sit by the window, and listen attentively, as if the bell and drum in Tianyin temple were ringing for him. There is another charm.

During his healing period, only FA Xiang and FA Shan often came to visit him in Tianyin temple. Almost all the other monks did not come, let alone master Puhong and other monks of Pu generation. And because of the injury. GUI Li has never been out of this room. Apart from occasionally opening the window and looking out, what he saw in front of him was just a small courtyard with red walls and blue tiles, and a few short trees planted in the courtyard.

It’s just for the ghost.

Such an ordinary and simple small yard had a familiar feeling that he had not seen for a long time. Although he did not reveal it from the day he opened the window, he immediately fell in love with this place in his heart.

Listening to the morning bell in the morning and listening to the evening drum in the evening, such a quiet and leisurely time, however, has made him unable to give up. Intoxicated.

Who knows, in his heart, once the biggest extravagant hope, but is to live such a peaceful life

Xumishan, Tianyin temple, the vast and magnificent temples, temples and pavilions, that strange and remote corner of the small courtyard, so live, live, live

“Cheep!” The wooden door was pushed open. FA Xiang came in alone, glanced at the room, and then fell on GUI Li, who was lying on the bed. He closed his eyes and didn’t know if he was asleep.

FA Xiang smiles. He turned around and closed the door and said, “how do you feel today? Is your chest still painful?”

The ghost Li body moved, slowly opened his eyes, looked at the Dharma one eye, and said faintly: “you should ask this sentence every time you come. Don’t you feel bored? "

FA Xiang smiles and shakes his head. His eyes turn, but he goes to the other side of the wall. In front of the picture of the statue of Avalokitesvara, he takes three sticks of sandalwood from the altar table, lights them on a candle beside it, and then inserts them into the copper incense burner.

The smoke curled up and drifted into the air. The statue of Guanyin suddenly became a little hazy, and the air began to smell of sandalwood.

Dharma Xianghe paid three obeisances to the image of Guanyin. Then he turned around and looked at the ghost for a long time. Suddenly he said, “are you just here to worship?”

He was stunned for a moment, and could not help but look at the portrait. In the picture, the grand master Guanyin has a compassionate, dignified and beautiful face. A pair of long and thin eyes, he seems to be looking at all the mortals in the world. At this moment, he is looking at himself as if he were merciful.

His heart moved, but then he sneered: “I worship her very much. If she really has a spirit, I don’t know how many times in the past I have sought God and the gods and Buddhas, and I have not seen them show mercy!”

FA Xiang looked at him for a long time. He looked at him calmly and fiercely. He still sneered at him and did not show any regret. After a long time, FA Xiangchang sighed and turned around. He bowed his head to the statue of Avalokitesvara and said something softly in his mouth.

Ghouli looked at his appearance behind him and sneered.

After the ceremony, the Dharma minister turned around, and the compassion on his face gradually faded. He put on a peaceful smile and said, “I think you look good today, and your body has generally recovered recently. Why don’t we go out?”

On hearing this, he was stunned and said, “go out, where are you going?”

FA Xiang said with a smile, “go where you want to go and meet the people you want to see.”

The ghost frowned, then raised his eyebrow and said, “why, is it master Pu Hong?”

The Dharma minister nodded his head and said, “yes, master, I’m very glad to hear that your body is recovering. Let me come and have a look today. If you are not tired, you can meet. I don’t know what you think? "

He glared at the Dharma for a long time, and then said with a smile: “OK, OK, OK, I have been waiting for this day for a long time. Naturally, I want to see him. Not to mention that I am well, I will climb to see him if he wants to.”

Dharma is consistent with the ten ways: “benefactor, please follow me.”

After that, he led the way, went to the door first, opened the door and went out.

However, when he was about to walk out of the room, he suddenly looked back at the picture of the statue of Guanyin hanging on the wall. In the light smoke, he was kind and smiling, and seemed to be staring at him.

The ghost frowned and snorted, but he turned around immediately, never looking back, and went straight away. Only the thin sandalwood was floating in the empty room behind him.

Walk out of the courtyard. It is a passage about two feet long, four feet wide, with red walls on both sides, and more than two people tall. The top is covered with green glazed tiles. At the end of the passage is a circular arch. When you approach that arch, you can hear a sound coming from outside.

The voice was rather strange. At first glance, it seemed to be the voice of monks in the temple chanting the Scriptures. But there are also other strange sounds, some of which should not appear here in the ghostly imagination. For example, village women get together to chat and talk, or the believers pay homage to the Buddha in high voice, and some children cry.

How can these strange sounds appear in the Tianyin temple, which is known as one of the three great sects of the right way in the world?

He looked at FA Xiang, but he saw that FA Xiang’s face did not change. He led the way in front of him and went out towards the arch. He frowned grimly. Calmed down, he went out.

Outside the door, you can see the white jade as the stone, the terrace as the field, and the stone steps, nine in a group, connecting to the Daxiong hall, which is as high as 9981 groups. Between the jade and stone railings, you can see that the palace is very tall. In front of the hall, 13 huge stone pillars rose from the sky, more than 10 Zhang high. The golden walls on the top of the hall are brilliant, and the eight ridges are equally distributed on it. Before each ridge and cornice dragon head, ten auspicious auspicious beasts are carved in different shapes and lifelike.

His Highness’s various carvings are gorgeous and exquisite, which is far beyond the imagination of the world. It can be made by people who are not waiting. Behind the Mahavira hall, on both sides and in front of it, there are high-rise halls connected with one another. In the meantime, either the square is connected, or the path is winding. Some of them are directly connected, and they are stacked in layers, which is very spectacular.

Although the building is magnificent and magnificent, it is really amazing. But at this moment, the most astonishing thing is not these things, but on the solemn Holy Land of Buddhism, there are countless mortals shuttling around, countless people holding incense, kneeling to worship Buddha, steps square, inside and outside the hall, which is unimaginable.

A huge Tianyin temple, which has a high status in the world’s right way, is like an ordinary temple in the world. It is open to countless secular people to burn incense and worship Buddha.

GUI Li never thought of this. He understood the strange sounds just now, but he was more confused about all the things in front of him. Growing up on Qingyun Mountain, he has long been used to the so-called fairy family style. The fairyland of Xianshan mountain was originally owned by only a Taoist. In Qingyun Mountain, where has ever seen a common people go up the mountain to burn incense and pray for wishes?

He turned his head and looked at him. He was surprised and asked, “this…”

With a smile, he said, “today is the first day of junior high school, so there are more people. Although there are hundreds of people who come to visit the temple on the 15th day, there is no such custom.

"

GUI Li shook his head, hesitated for a moment, and finally asked, “no, I wonder. How can you let the people come in to burn incense and worship Buddha?”

It seemed to be expected that FA relative GUI Li would ask this question. He nodded his head, made a posture of walking this way, and then walked to the back of the hall of Mahavira, walking along the way: “in fact, Tianyin temple was not open to the secular world, just like the gate of Qingyun, but after my master Pu Hong took over the position of abbot, he and three other martial uncles learned Buddhism and made great wishes “Buddha is the Buddha of all living beings, not one of us. So he decided to open the mountain gate to accept the people. "

At this point, the Dharma minister stopped and pointed to the countless steps leading to the Mahavira hall and said, “do you see that long stone step?”

He nodded his head and said, “how?”

The Dharma is consistent with the ten principles: “that was when a martial uncle saw that the mountain road was steep. Although the people were willing to worship Buddha, there were many weak people who could not move on the mountain, so they could not go up the mountain to fulfill their wishes. With great magic power, with the power of one person, it took ten years of hard work to carve out this smooth path of Buddha sea on the originally steep mountain road, and did such boundless good deeds.”

GUI Li couldn’t help but be awed, and his face began to rise again. He said, “there is such a great master. May I ask his name?”

FA Xiang looked at him, and after an unexpected silence for a moment, he whispered, “that martial uncle, whose name is Puzhi, has been dead for more than ten years.”

His fierce and stiff body was like the word “Puzhi”, which was like thunder on a sunny day. Shengsheng hit him in his mind, shaking his mind to pieces.

FA Xiang looked at the ghost and saw that it was changeable and changeable. He sighed and said in a low voice: “well, let’s go. The abbot is still waiting for us.”

He walked with FA Xiang, but he was relaxed. Now it has become very heavy. After walking for several Zhangs, he suddenly turned back and saw the crowd in the distance, countless people walking on the stone steps. The old man, the man, the woman, the child, one by one, walked on the stone steps with reverent faces, chanting the name of Buddha in their mouths, as if they had gone this way, they were closer to the Buddha.

The expression on his face is complex and hard to understand. After a long time, he turned his head slowly and walked forward. The Dharma that is waiting in front of him is ten chanting Buddhas, but he doesn’t say much.

They went together, leaving behind the countless believers and the silent Buddhist Road, and left them in the world.

This place was originally the human world, and it is no longer the realm of immortal Buddhism.

After passing the Mahavira hall, there is still a long string of temples. After all, Tianyin temple is a famous school, which can be compared with other temples. However, FA Xiang walked backward with the ghost and Li all the way, but did not stop at any of the temples and pavilions, just walked back to the mountain.

All the way, GUI Li just followed FA Xiang without saying a word. His face was full of thoughts. He turned a blind eye to the beautiful buildings around him.

Although Xumi mountain is not as high as Qingyun gate Tongtian peak, it is definitely not low. The Tianyin temple they just came out of was in the middle of the mountain, but they went up this time and walked for half an hour before they saw the plaque of the small Tianyin temple.

However, it is far away from the secular world. You can see the green pines and bamboos around the temple. The wind blows through the mountains and the bamboo shakes. It’s indescribable and elegant. Compared with the bustle at the foot of the mountain, it’s a different taste.

Walking into chastity, as like as two peas, the spirit of the Buddhist temple was almost the same as the one in the Buddhist temple. But now the world is full of righteousness.

The catastrophe started in spring and summer of that year. Thousands of years later, people in the world still remember clearly that period of horrible and crazy days.

In the extreme south of Southern Xinjiang, among the 100000 mountains, countless monsters suddenly swarmed out. They were bloodthirsty, and they killed people whenever they saw people. There were also many evil beasts who were greedy for human flesh, and the places they passed were appalling.

The catastrophe broke out in southern Xinjiang, which was close to 100000 mountains, and quickly spread to the whole southern Xinjiang. Miao, Zhuang, Tu, Li and Gaoshan of the five ethnic groups in southern Xinjiang rose to resist.

However, in the face of numerous alien monsters, especially in the numerous ferocious ones, there are more than a dozen strange monsters with extremely strong Demon power and witchcraft skills. The resistance of the five clans is no different from that of a mantis. In a blink of an eye, they are defeated, and the lives of the southern Xinjiang people are in ruins.

The incident immediately shocked the world and spread all over the world. The Chinese people in the Middle Kingdom were in constant panic every day. Some of them, who were close to southern Xinjiang, fled to the north with their families and families. They only hoped that they could be as far away from the catastrophe as possible.

All the monks in the world were shocked. Even Zhengmo Er Dao, who had always been fighting openly and secretly, stopped to watch the South and began to figure out his own countermeasures.

The incense burning Valley, located in the southern orthodox school, was lucky to escape because the valley master Yun Yilan happened to lead most of his disciples to visit daoxuan immortal in Qingyun Mountain.

After the incident, the leader of Yunyi Lan Valley, who was in Qingyun Mountain, heard about the miserable situation of the people in southern Xinjiang. He felt very sad and said that if he was there, he would never allow evil spirits to do evil to the people. The words are very sad and self reproachful. He has already committed suicide to thank the whole world. Fortunately, the left and right disciples held on and the elders of Qingyun gate offered their best advice. Then the cloud Valley master calmed down and vowed to do his best to burn the whole valley of incense Valley and avenge this deep blood feud for the people of Southern Xinjiang!

Not long ago, Yun Yilan announced to the people who were practicing Taoism in Qingyun Mountain, indicating that today’s catastrophe was actually caused by an animal demon, and the demon’s skill is high. Because of its ferocious nature, it cannot be resisted by the common attack of the whole world. In view of this, the burning incense Valley and Qingyun gate jointly appeal to the world’s practitioners. Take the power of the world and kill this Liao!

The next day, Tianyin temple, which received the news, formally responded and agreed with the call of Qingyun and incense burning, and soon sent people to join the alliance.

Zhengdao was eager to raise funds for discussion, and sent a number of excellent disciples to investigate the details of these monsters. After all, if you know yourself and know the enemy, you will be invincible.

On the day before the arrival of the summer solstice, this strange calm was finally broken, and the monsters who ravaged Southern Xinjiang to the point where they could not look like were finally killed in the Middle Earth.

However, at the beginning, the death and injury of the people were not very serious, because as early as a month ago, the people near the South had already run clean. It’s just that the number of these monsters seems to be increasing. It spread even more rapidly, and was about to be forced into the hinterland of the Middle Earth. At that time, it was the time for all the people in the world to fall into a miserable place.

Yesterday, it said that a village was bloodwashed, and today it is rumored that the whole city has been turned into ruins. Every day spent in fear and fear is so miserable and panic for anyone.

It’s just that for people who are dying. Even if all the people in the world are dead, it seems that it’s none of your business.

Like the passage of the netherworld, a huge whirlpool of dark and invisible bottom hangs upside down in the sky, like the nine hell demons opening their horrible mouths to devour everything in the world. The wind is strong, and the clouds are whirling. Thunder rumbled, electricity darted.

Zhang Xiaofan deceiving himself to fly in, the dark light of the fire staff flickers, which is noticeable under the dark clouds. Lu Xueqi looks at Zhang Xiaofan’s figure in the green light, and her jade face is white.

“The true secret of magic sword against thunder” is the most marvelous skill of Taoist immortal method. It can be imagined that Lu Xueqi’s body is under great pressure at the moment when he causes the heaven and the earth to be so awesome with his mortal body. “Tianya” is an immortal magic weapon. It was originally a good weapon to use the magic sword to resist thunder. But compared with it, Lu Xueqi’s Taoist practice was insufficient.

At the moment, she only felt that in the dark clouds of the sky, infinite power was pouring into her body like surging waves. It seemed that there was no change in the whole body, but the blood in her body was almost broken by this great force. If it wasn’t for Tianya to absorb the surging force, Lu Xueqi would not have been able to support her for a long time.

The wind roared and thunder roared. She stood up in the air. In a trance, she almost thought that she was like a weak grass in the wind. Next moment, she remembered the words of master Shuiyue passing on her magic skill: “Xueqi, you have the best qualification, which is the only one I’ve seen in my life, but the true formula is too powerful, so the power of counter eating is even more inescapable. The day of your cultivation is still shallow. Although you can barely master it, you must not practice at will to avoid the disaster of extinction. "

“Boom!”

A thunder, almost from the top of Tongtian peak to the sky, everyone faintly felt that the ground under his feet shook slightly, as if the ancient god of thunder was disturbed and fell asleep, roaring furiously!

Everyone changes color at one time!

Zhang Xiaofan is only two Zhangs away from Lu Xueqi at the moment. After seeing this power, everyone knows that once Lu Xueqi’s practice is completed, he will be annihilated for fear. But all of a sudden he was so tight that he stopped as if he had hit a soft wall and could not move forward.

Zhang Xiaofan’s face was dead in an instant. “The true secret of magic sword against thunder” is one of the wonders of Qingyun gate. How marvelous is it? During the process of casting, Zhang Xiaofan can’t enter it because the magic soldiers naturally put a layer of invisible shield around the caster.

The staff is more brilliant, but it can’t move forward any more.

Maybe in the power of spirit, Zhang Xiaofan’s fire staff is not inferior to Tianya, but in the skill, it’s too far away. He just uses his own spirit to urge the fire staff to have a fever, which is definitely not comparable to Lu Xueqi’s unique skill, which has been practiced by generations of ancestors of Qingyun sect for thousands of years.

But at this moment of desperation, seeing the huge whirlpool in the sky rotating faster, thunder and lightning making, the light of Tianya divine sword is more and more bright, this peerless immortal method is about to be completed, Lu Xueqi suddenly shakes, her originally white face turns red instantly, and “wow” spews out a big mouth of blood, almost becoming a blood mist in front of her.

When Tianya divine sword appeared, it seemed that it was unsteady. Lu Xueqi clenched her silver teeth, closed her eyes, and concentrated all her heart and spirit on Tianya. After a while, the light of Tianya stabilized, but it was more brilliant than before, and could not be forced to look.

There was a loud noise in the dark cloud, and there seemed to be a bright light in the deepest part of the huge whirlpool. It was a myriad of lightning converging into one, which was faintly facing the Tianya sword in Lu Xueqi’s hand.

However, Lu Xueqi’s heart was a burst of despair. In the wind, there was a sharp roar indeed. She protects Tianya with all her strength, but she can’t take care of her body guard any more. Zhang Xiaofan is so happy that he and the fire staff turn into a dark and green light column, cross the sky, and rush to the beautiful woman who sways in the wind.

Is that it?

Is it all up here?

Her mind suddenly calmed down, thinking so lightly in that moment.

This moment, a short moment, the world is quiet, frozen, all things are fixed there, only she stood in the wind, clothes fluttering, black hair blowing, opened the closed eyes, looking at the way ahead of the blue light.

That moment seems eternal!

Zhang Xiaofan saw her, and her eyes!

She stood alone in the wind and rain, but she was so peaceful in the face of the great power of heaven and earth, but her face was slightly pale, and there was a sadness and a sense of panic in her eyes.

Wind and rain howl, desolate world, this beautiful woman, and he quietly look at each other.

Deep! deeply!

Zhang Xiaofan suddenly smiled, with a point of sadness and heart death, as if last night.

The fire staff melted into the light of the God sword of Tianya. No one could see them any more, and the light of the fire staff suddenly faded. At this moment, the sky is loud, and a huge electric pole falls from the sky to the sky.

The whole heaven and earth, full of gods and Buddhas, seem to sing together at the same time.

The huge light column refracted from Tianya, took the momentum of destroying the sky and destroying the earth, and rushed to Zhang Xiaofan. At the critical moment of life and death, the fire stick rose up in the air and blocked in front of the master.

Next moment, Zhang Xiaofan is swallowed by the light.

Long time! Long time! Long time!

People stared at the sky, looked at the young man, tightly holding a black fire stick, like a stone suffering from all kinds of scars, and fell straight down.

But he would never walk alone in the darkness, but there was no way to go except the darkness.

So he was sad and indignant, and deeply felt that there was a burning fire, so he swore to the nine Ghosts: even if his body and soul were turned into ashes together, he would light up this light, even if he buried all the things in the world together.

Since ancient times, that trace of violence has been as rebellious as ever!

Zhang Xiaofan slowly opened his eyes.

The soft light came into his eyes, and the familiar taste of the house floated in the room.

No one seems to be here.

He slowly sat up, just want to lift his hand to wipe off a little sweat on his forehead, then he only felt a sharp pain in his shoulder, chest and abdomen. At that time, he took a breath of cool air and turned white with pain.

He sat on the bed, dare not move again, after a long time, the pain slowly dissipated.

At this time, it’s afternoon. The door of the room is open, and two windows are set up. You can see the green grass and bamboo in the courtyard. The little grey who has always followed him and the rhubarb who has always followed him are gone. Will they find the flesh and bones again?

He smiled at the empty room and smiled at himself.

“Squeak!” the door opened, and Su Ru came in. Zhang Xiaofan moved and called “teacher’s mother”. Before he got up, his face began to twitch again.

Su Ru quickly walked to the bedside and sat down. In a soft voice, she said, “don’t move, Xiao Fan.”

Zhang Xiaofan waited for the pain to subside, and then said to Su Ru, “I didn’t know that you came…”

Su Ru took a look at him and said, “most of your life has gone, but you still have the mind to remember this! Stop talking nonsense and sit down. "

Zhang Xiaofan smiles, Su Ru checks for him, nods and says, “your injury is almost as good as before, but the damage of meridians and collaterals in your body is too serious, so it’s impossible to rest uneasily.”

Zhang Xiaofan said, “yes, I have disgraced Shifu and Shiniang. That’s right…”

Su Ru cut off: “you have made a big face for your master. In the past three hundred years, there is no better disciple in dazhufeng than you except for the seven pulse meeting in which your master participated.”

Zhang Xiaofan blushed and bowed his head and said, “that’s all good luck for the disciples.”

Su Ru smiled and patted him on the shoulder. Zhang Xiaofan immediately thought of it, and said, “is the contest over? Who won the championship in the end? Is that elder martial sister Lu?”

Su Ru shook her head slightly and said, “no, it’s Qi Hao from dragon head peak.”

Zhang Xiaofan didn’t know why. Suddenly, he felt sad and said in a low voice, “it’s elder martial brother Qi. He’s really powerful. Even elder martial sister Lu, who owns Tianya, was defeated by him.”

Su Ru seemed to be touched by what he said. She sighed low and said, “you’ve been hurt badly this time. Your master took great pains to cure you. Listen to him. Although Lu Xueqi’s practice is not enough, if it’s not for you to burn or burn… Your magic weapon will stop you, even if it’s not for the immortals It’s back to the sky. "

Zhang Xiaofan listened to her words, and suddenly remembered that, looking around, he couldn’t find the black ugly fire stick.

Su Ru looked at him and said lightly, “your magic weapon has been taken by your master.”

Zhang Xiaofan said in a low voice, “yes.” Then he couldn’t help asking again, “master, he is an old man…”

Su Ru said: “you have been in a coma for five days and five nights, and the injury was not stable until last night. This morning, the head elder martial brother of tongtianfeng sent a letter to ask your master to go to tongtianfeng. You should be in tongtianfeng now.”

Zhang Xiaofan nodded slowly. He didn’t know what it was like. He thought it should be OK. But for the first time in the past two years, the fire stick left him, but he always felt a little lost.

Su Ru took a look at him, as if there was a strange look in her eyes, but she said: “you just woke up, don’t be too tired, and have a lot of rest. I have told them not to disturb you. I will send you the three meals

Zhang Xiaofan said, “thank you very much, Shiniang.”

Su Ru nodded and said, “then you have a rest. I’ll ask Bing Shu to deliver the food later.” Then she turned back and walked out. Just as she was about to walk out of the door, she suddenly heard Zhang Xiaofan shouting behind her:

“Shiniang.”

Su Ru turned around and said, “what?”

Zhang Xiaofan looked at her as if he hesitated for a moment, and then said, “I want to ask you, teacher, what’s the result of the contest of Lin Jingyu of longshoufeng? At that time, when I was on Tongtian peak, I really had no time to ask him.”

Su Ru looked at him again and said, “he was in the top eight, but he was defeated by Qi Hao, his senior brother.”

Zhang Xiaofan was stunned for a moment and said, “he, too… Thank you, Shiniang.”

Su Ru shook her head slightly and said, “you have a rest.” Then he turned and walked out.

Zhang Xiaofan slowly lay down, looking at the ceiling of the room, silent.

Note: Li nationality is a branch of Baiyue in ancient times. Before Qin and Han Dynasties, a branch of Luoyue crossed the sea from the mainland to Hainan Island. Today, they mainly live in the Li and Miao Autonomous Prefecture in central and southern Hainan Province, and the rest live in Wanning, Tunchang, Qionghai, Chengmai, Lu County, Ding’an and other counties.

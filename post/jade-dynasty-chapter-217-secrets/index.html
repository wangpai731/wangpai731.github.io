However, there is a faint pink in the snow white, such as the beautiful flowers blooming quietly in the deep mountains and valleys.

On both sides of the stone path, tall pruning bamboos sway slightly. On the green bamboo leaves, there are condensation dew drops, which glide quietly and fly to the earth.

She stepped forward and never looked back. She walked into the deep green bamboo forest.

The stone path is deep and zigzag, and the bright light of the early morning penetrates through the dense gaps in the bamboo forest, and the bamboo shadow flickers gently, reflecting her graceful posture.

In front of him, a simple bamboo house is gradually emerging. It is the place where the first master of water moon in xiaozhufeng is meditating on his daily life.

Lu Xueqi stopped at the door, hesitated for a moment, and patted the bamboo door.

“Master, I’ll see you later.

"

Lu Xueqi went to master Shuiyue in silence, knelt down and said in a low voice, “master, here comes the disciple.”

Master Shuiyue patted the bamboo bed beside him and said, “you can sit down too.”

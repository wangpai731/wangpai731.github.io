With the end of the first round, we ushered in the rest time of three sticks of incense. At this moment, all the disciples on the north bank were furious, staring at the south bank, staring at Bai Xiaochun!

They are waiting for the second round to start. They want to use all means to defeat Bai Xiaochun. Even if the north bank wins this time, they don’t pay attention to it. At the moment, they only focus on how to wash the former shame!

“Bai Xiaochun, in this second round, we must let you know that you bring shame to us on the north shore, and we will make you pay a heavy price!”

“This kind of person should be knocked down by a group of war animals, and only then can he get rid of his hatred. This second round will make him sad all his life!”

“Down with Bai Xiaochun, down with this shameless man!”

The voice of the north bank heard that they wanted to kill Bai Xiaochun’s heart. At the moment, all the eight people gathered in front of them. Except for the ghost teeth who closed their eyes calmly, the other seven were all grinding their fists.

They knew that they represented the will of all the disciples on the north bank. At this moment, when they looked at Bai Xiaochun, they all had a strong light in their eyes, and they were also analyzing each other’s hearts. They were thinking about the details of Bai Xiaochun’s war just now. Gradually, they all had their own bottom in their hearts and found a way to restrain them.

“Bai Xiaochun just has pills. As long as you don’t give him a chance to take out pills, everything will be enough.”

“It’s just to win with pills. Without pills, it’s easy to kill him. In this second round, he’ll lose, and he’ll lose miserably!”

“Too much!” Bai Xiaochun’s heart is full of grievances. He thinks that the north bank is too bullying. He won a fight, but he was so cruel He wanted to say something, but he was afraid that he would blow up when he spoke.

While the north bank was agitated, the south bank also made an unwilling voice. When the momentum was gradually raised, Ouyang Jie’s words suddenly came out.

They left their hands in an instant and ran straight to the ball to blend into it.

It can be clearly seen that after these beads are integrated into the ball, the number marked on them is replaced by one to ten, and one bead is blank.

Eleven beads kept spinning in it, faster and faster, until the ball became blurred when it could not be seen clearly.

“With the mysterious technique, we cover up the mystery of heaven and seek fairness. Even I can’t interfere with the beads in the ball, so you can rest assured.

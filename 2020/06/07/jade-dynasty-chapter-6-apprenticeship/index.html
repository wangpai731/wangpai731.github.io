Zhang Xiaofan woke up wandering for a long while, slowly sat up, and the past was like a tide, and his heart surged for a moment.

A nightmare!

“You wake up, that’s fine.” A voice came from the door and walked in.

Zhang Xiaofan looked up and recognized Song Daren, who had been seen on Tongtian Peak at the time, tall and rude. In his current state of mind, somehow, seeing this acquaintance is somewhat kind.

“Brother Song.” Zhang Xiaofan called out.

Zhang Xiaofan stayed for a while and said, “Which family?”

Song Daren smiled and reiterated Tian Nanyi’s acceptance of him as a disciple. Of course, in the Yuqing Hall of Tongtianfeng that day, he didn’t know the little dispute between the elders of Qingyunmen.

Zhang Xiaofan heard it, and was at a loss for a while. Qingyunmen was really a first-class person with a fairy in his mind like a farmer’s child. He never dreamed that one day, he would also have the opportunity to enter Qingyunmen. However, this price is not what he is willing to pay.

He gritted his teeth, and after all he knew it would be useless, he opened his mouth and said: “Brother Song.”

Song Daren nodded with a smile and said, “Good. Brother Xiao, you have been sleeping all day and night, maybe you are hungry?”

Song Daren said with a smile: “Come on, Brother Xiao, let’s go eat something first. By the way, I will tell you something about the situation, and then I will go to see Master and your mother together, and you have met other brothers.”

Zhang Xiaofan nodded and got out of bed, only to notice that the room he was in was quite similar to the place where Qingyun disciples lived on Tongtianfeng, but it seemed to be more spacious.

In order to take him out, Song Daren said, “Our Dazhufeng is no different from other people. The people are very thin. Zhang Xiaofan’s child’s heart is full of smile, and his sadness is much lighter. Seeing that the brothers are so kind, the fear of fear in the future has gradually settled down.

The Qingyun Mountains are towering, the tigers are in the Central Plains, there is a big river “Hongchuan” in the mountain shade, and “Heyang City” in the important town of Shanyang. The geographical position is very important.

There are hundreds of miles of mountains in Qingyun Mountain, and the peaks are ups and downs, with seven peaks at the highest, towering into the clouds. On weekdays, I can see white clouds surrounding the mountainside, and I don’t know the true beauty of the top. Qingyun Mountain is densely covered with forests, waterfalls and strange rocks, and rare birds and beasts.

It’s just more famous, but it is Qingyun Gate, a cultivation school on this mountain.

Qingyun has a long history. It has been more than 2,000 years since the founding of the school. It is said that the Kaipai ancestor was originally a river and lake master, and he was down for half his life, unhappy. When he was forty-nine years old, he traveled all over the world, passing Qingyun Mountain, and at a glance he saw that the mountain was full of spirits and spirits, and it was a wonderful place. Immediately climbing at the moment, dining style, drinking dew, practicing Daoist practice, it was not much. In a dense cave deep in Qingyun Mountain, I got a nameless scroll, uploaded all kinds of magic tricks, difficult and dry, but it is infinitely powerful and powerful. huge.

Xiang Shi got this adventure and concentrated on practicing. Twenty years later, little success was achieved, but out of the wind and rain, although it could not dominate the world, it became a hero. Then on the Qingyun Mountain, the school was founded and named: Qingyun. Therefore, in the scroll, close to Taoism, he dressed up as a Taoist, self-proclaimed “Qingyunzi”, and many later generations called “Qingyunzhenren”.

Qingyun Zishou is 367 years old. He received ten disciples before his death. He told him before his death: “I have been studying for half of my life, and I am good at the art of feng shui. This Qingyun Mountain is a rare place on earth. I have a mountain in Qingyun. If there is this mountain, it will definitely flourish in the future, and you must never give up. Remember, remember!”

At that time, the ten disciples nodded one after another, convinced that Qingyunzi died. Unexpectedly, in the next 100 years, I didn’t know that it was God’s will, or it was simply that the Qingyunzi technique was not perfect. The Qingyun Gate not only did not develop, but it gradually declined.

Of the ten disciples, two died prematurely, four died in a vendetta against each other, and the remaining one was crippled and one was missing. After 50 years of this, there was an unprecedented natural disaster earthquake in Qingyun Mountain, the floods broke out, the mountains were shaken, and there were countless deaths and injuries. The only seedling left is limited to qualifications. The skills are low, and the scenery of Qingyunzi was no longer there. Instead, because of the ancient scroll, it attracted foreign enemies to fight for, and several bloody battles. If it were not for the powerful magic weapons of Qingyunzi, I’m afraid the Qingyun Gate has been destroyed.

This situation has lasted for four hundred years. The Qingyun Gate has no improvement, and it can almost be described as “Guoyan Residual Puff”. In the end, he was even bullied to his doorstep. Among the seven peaks in Qingyun, except the main peak, Tongtianfeng, the remaining six were occupied by foreign enemies, including robbers and robbers, who used them as strongholds, looted everywhere, and ran wild. Uninformed people often misunderstand that Qingyunmen has fallen into such a state. Although Qingyun’s children have justified their defense, they also have the intention to kill the enemy, but they are weak and lamentable. I still remember that it was really the most tragic period of Qingyun’s veins.

The situation did not change until 1,300 years ago.

Probably Qing Yunzi’s physiognomy finally appeared, or he was tired from heaven, and he no longer made fun of Qingyun Gate. At this time, from the eleventh generation of Qingyunmen’s descendants, there was an amazing peerless leader who had a brilliant leadership. Character —- Aoba Taoist. The surname of Ye Ye, who was originally a poor scholar, was talented and clever, but he failed to make repeated attempts. He had a chance, and was accepted as a closed disciple for the tenth generation head of Qingyun Gate, only 22 years old.

After the introduction of Aoba, within a year, he realized all the swordsmanship skills taught by No Fangzi and took the lead among the disciples. Another year later, even no prescription can only barely draw a tie with him by virtue of deep practice. Fang Zi was shocked and happy, and he took out the ancient scroll passed down by his ancestor and passed it on to Aoba to participate in his own auspiciousness. Aoba then closed the gate at the “Magic Moon Cave” at the back mountain of Tongtian Peak. It was only 13 years before this barrier was broken.

It is said that when he broke through, it was the night of the full moon. The cold moon hung high that night, and the whole Qingyun Mountain peaked like a day. Suddenly, there was a violent wind, and there was Long Yin and Chang Xiao in the back mountain, and the sound was shocking for hundreds of miles, and the listener did not change color. Later, there was a light purple auspicious light, rising into the sky, a loud noise, the hall of the Moon Moon Cave suddenly opened, the green leaves must be white, smiling, with a clear light, slowly came out, everyone was horrified, thought it was a fairy.

After that, Aoba officially became a monk, taking the name “Ao” of Qingyun under his family name of Ye, hence the name Aoba. He smiled at the same day and said, “Master, wait a moment, the disciple will go out to do things, and he will return within a day.”

Everyone was unclear. Therefore, one day and one night, the green leaves Yujian returned, and the enemy of the six peaks of Qingyun Mountain had already uttered all the fuss. Qingye Dao’s humane law is strong, and the means are ruthless. For a time, the world became famous, and the Qingyun Gate was in full swing.

Another year later, Wu Fang Zi was about to pass the position of the head to Aoba, and he went to repair himself, and he no longer took care of the chores in the door. After Aoba took power, he worked hard to help his fellow students, carefully selected his descendants, and when he learned from the nameless scrolls, he had unpredictable powers. The Qingyun Gate has flourished since then. For fifty years, it has been the pillar of the right path, and after two hundred years, it has led the various schools of the right path.

Ao Ye, Gao Shou, died at the age of seven hundred and fifty. He was rigorous in his life and only passed seven people. And the head of the Taoist Xuanzheng people, the merits of creation, the extraordinary saints, are even more peerless in the world.
